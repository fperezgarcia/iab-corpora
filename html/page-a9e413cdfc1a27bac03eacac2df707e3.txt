						<div class="date">August 22, 2013</div>					</div>

						<div class="has_comments"><a href="/nissan-patrol-guinness-world-record-towing/28806/#comments">14 Comments</a></div> 						<div class="has_pictures"><a href="/nissan-patrol-guinness-world-record-towing/28806/pictures">3 Pictures</a></div>					</div>

			<div class="summary">
						
				<p>                Airplanes seem to be the default standard when automakers want to demonstrate the pulling power of a particular vehicle. Nissan has stuck with this yardstick in using its Patrol 4WD to pull a fully-laden Ilyushin Il-76 cargo plane weighing 170.9 tons over 50 m to set a new Guinness World Record.				 &nbsp;<a href="/nissan-patrol-guinness-world-record-towing/28806/" class="readmore_link">Read More</a>				</p>
