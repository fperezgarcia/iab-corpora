				<div class="KonaBody"> 

					<p><span id="intelliTXT">Buick today introduced the all-new 2013 Encore &ndash; a luxury small  crossover that delivers Buick&rsquo;s signature style, interior acoustic  refinement and collection of luxury amenities in a maneuverable,  versatile and efficient package.<div id="articleAd">
<!-- FM Medium Rectangle Zone -->
<script type='text/javascript' src='http://static.fmpub.net/zone/227'></script>
<!-- FM Medium Rectangle Zone -->
</div>   <p>The Encore is on display at the North American International Auto  Show in Detroit through Jan. 22, and goes on sale in early 2013. <br /><br /><img width="650" height="433" src="/images/users/Agent009/images/001-2013-buick-encore-1326120425.jpg" alt="" /></p>  <p>&ldquo;Buick Encore is a great-looking, smart choice for those who want  luxury crossover amenities in a more maneuverable and agile vehicle,&rdquo;  said Tony DiSalle, U.S. vice president of Buick Marketing. &ldquo;It  represents a luxury alternative for a new audience of Buick customers,  and delivers features and flexibility not typically found in vehicles of  its size today.&rdquo;</p>  <p>Buick models continue to gain in popularity, with two consecutive  years of higher retail sales. The Encore joins Verano &ndash; Buick&rsquo;s third  all-new sedan in three years &ndash; to round out the brand&rsquo;s lineup and  continues the momentum established by the Enclave crossover almost five  years ago. <br /><br /><img width="650" height="433" src="/images/users/Agent009/images/006-2013-buick-encore-1326120428.jpg" alt="" /><br />&nbsp;</p>  <p>With room for five and up to 48.4 cubic feet (1,372 liters) of  storage space with the rear seat folded, the Encore offers substantial  cargo capacity along with the confident, higher driving position,  all-wheel-drive capability and contemporary styling of a crossover. In  fact, its cargo space compares favorably against similar-sized  international competitors:</p>  <table align="center" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="0">   <tbody><tr>     <td width="169" valign="top"><p align="center">&nbsp;</p></td>     <td width="216" valign="top"><p align="center"><strong> Rear Seat in Place </strong></p></td>     <td width="234" valign="top"><p align="center"><strong> Rear Seat Folded </strong></p></td>   </tr>   <tr>     <td width="169" valign="top"><p align="center">Buick Encore</p></td>     <td width="216" valign="top"><p align="center">18.8 cu. ft. / 533 liters</p></td>     <td width="234" valign="top"><p align="center">48.4 cu. ft. / 1,372 liters</p></td>   </tr>   <tr>     <td width="169" valign="top"><p align="center">BMW X1</p></td>     <td width="216" valign="top"><p align="center">16.9 cu. ft. / 480 liters</p></td>     <td width="234" valign="top"><p align="center">47.4 cu. ft. / 1,350 liters</p></td>   </tr>   <tr>     <td width="169" valign="top"><p align="center">Audi Q3</p></td>     <td width="216" valign="top"><p align="center">16.2 cu. ft. / 460 liters</p></td>     <td width="234" valign="top"><p align="center">48.2 cu. ft. / 1,365 liters</p></td>   </tr> </tbody></table>  <p>Additional features include 10 standard air bags and an Ecotec 1.4L  turbo four-cylinder/six-speed automatic powertrain, along with standard  and available technologies such as Buick&rsquo;s voice-activated IntelliLink &ndash;  which uses Bluetooth or a USB to connect the driver&rsquo;s smartphone to a  high-resolution, full-color seven-inch LCD display radio. <br /><br /><img width="650" height="366" src="/images/users/Agent009/images/007-2013-buick-encore-1326120429.jpg" alt="" /></p>  <p>And because it&rsquo;s a Buick, QuietTuning is standard on the Encore,  which also features Buick&rsquo;s first use of active noise cancellation  technology.</p>  <p><strong> Contemporary design offers versatile spaciousness </strong></p> <p>In keeping with the global design language that has come to  symbolize the brand, the Encore&rsquo;s sculpted and elegant styling traits &ndash;  including sweeping character lines &ndash; make it instantly identifiable as a  contemporary Buick. Signature cues include a waterfall grille, chrome  accents and surrounds, portholes on the hood and painted lower panels,  similar to the Enclave. <br /><br /><img width="650" height="437" src="/images/users/Agent009/images/011-2013-buick-encore.jpg" alt="" /></p>  <p>&ldquo;The Encore brings Buick&rsquo;s timeless, sculptural design to the  crossover segment in a way that blends elegance, efficiency and  utility,&rdquo; said David Lyon, executive director of design. &ldquo;There&rsquo;s  confidence in its athletic stance and elemental beauty in its rich  details.&rdquo;</p>  <p>Blue-accented projector-beam headlamps and detailed taillamps  enhance the premium appearance; and 18-inch, five-spoke painted aluminum  wheels are standard. Seven-spoke chrome wheels are available. <br /><br /><img width="650" height="403" src="/images/users/Agent009/images/012-2013-buick-encore.jpg" alt="" /></p>  <p>Inside, the Encore&rsquo;s organic styling carries over to a flowing  instrument panel that wraps naturally into the door panels. A prominent  central instrument panel houses the large, full-color, seven-inch LCD  display for the IntelliLink voice-activated radio system. It also  features displays for the SiriusXM satellite radio, as well as available  navigation and rear camera systems. <br /><br /><img width="650" height="433" src="/images/users/Agent009/images/013-2013-buick-encore.jpg" alt="" /></p>  <p>A contemporary mix of dark and light tones distinguishes the cabin,  which is accented with ice blue ambient lighting, select use of bright  trim and wood-grain trim. Premium seats featuring high-density foam for  greater comfort on long drives are trimmed with either a contemporary  cloth/leatherette motif or available leather. Heated front seats and a  heated steering wheel are available. The rear seat features a fold-down  armrest with cup holders.</p> <p>More of the Encore&rsquo;s comfort and luxury features &ndash; standard and available &ndash; include:</p> <ul><li>Remote vehicle start</li><li>Dual-zone automatic climate control</li><li>Rainsense wipers</li><li>Bose premium sound system</li><li>Heated, leather-wrapped steering wheel with audio controls</li><li>Six-way, power-adjustable driver&rsquo;s seat with power lumbar adjustment and driver memory.</li></ul>  <p>Encore also features plenty of storage capacity and storage  compartments. It offers 18.8 cubic feet (533 liters) of storage behind  the split-folding rear seat and 48.4 cubic feet (1,372 liters) with the  rear seat folded. With the rear seat in place, there&rsquo;s room for at least  six full grocery bags and with the rear seat folded, there&rsquo;s room for  golf clubs and overnight bags for a weekend getaway. Even the front  passenger seat folds flat to extend the cargo length and accommodate  long items that would not normally fit in a vehicle of this size.</p>  <p>Nine exterior colors and three interior color combinations will be offered.</p>  <p><strong> QuietTuning gets &lsquo;active&rsquo; </strong></p> <p>QuietTuning is the process of defining Buick quality, sound and  vibration characteristics and designing and refining the vehicle  following a three-point strategy of reducing sounds at their source,  blocking sounds from entering the cabin and absorbing any remaining  sounds.</p>  <p>For the Encore, specific content was applied to achieve 40 sound  benchmarks for refinement, including Buick&rsquo;s first application of active  noise cancellation. With it, ceiling-mounted microphones detect engine  noise, the frequencies of which are processed by a computer that directs  counteracting sound waves through the audio system&rsquo;s speakers and  subwoofer. <br /><br /><img width="650" height="366" src="/images/users/Agent009/images/009-2013-buick-encore.jpg" alt="" /></p>  <p>Additional standard QuietTuning features include:</p> <ul><li>Acoustically treated headliner with a tuned gap between it and the high-strength-steel roof panel</li><li>Sound-absorbing materials used in the cabin, under the hood and in the wheel house liners</li><li>HydraMatic 6T40 six-speed transmission with neutral gear disengagement</li><li>Quiet-tuned 18-inch Continental tires</li><li>Aero-optimized outside mirrors</li><li>Ultra-quiet engine alternator</li><li>Acoustic-laminated windshield and thicker 5mm side window glass</li><li>Foam baffle insulation at the opening of body structure cavities.</li></ul>  <p><strong> &lsquo;One-tire&rsquo; philosophy sharpens ride and handling characteristics </strong></p> <p>The new Encore is built on a global small-crossover platform and  engineered with a &ldquo;one-tire&rdquo; philosophy. Everything is optimized around a  single 18-inch tire specification, designed for more precise suspension  tuning, driving refinement, quietness and a feeling of control. <br /><br /><img width="650" height="433" src="/images/users/Agent009/images/005-2013-buick-encore-1326120428.jpg" alt="" /></p>  <p>The Encore&rsquo;s 100.6-inch (2,555 mm) wheelbase and wide stance &ndash;  60.6-inch (1,540 mm) front and rear tracks &ndash; contribute to a more  confident-feeling driving experience, while a strong structure enables  greater suspension precision and a quieter ride. Tuned engine, including  two hydraulic mounts, are tailored to the engine&rsquo;s inherent torque axis  to disrupt the transmission of vibration and noise. This configuration  also helps control the transfer of momentum, resulting in improved  dynamic balance.</p>  <p>A MacPherson strut front suspension is used with coil springs, a  large stabilizer bar and side-load-compensated front strut modules. The  rear suspension incorporates a compound crank (torsion beam) design,  with a double-wall, tubular V-shape beam profile with gas-charged  twin-tube shocks. It combines all the advantages of a conventional  torsion beam axle, including minimal space requirements, with low weight  and consistent camber control. Urethane spring isolators are used to  dampen noise.</p>  <p>The Encore also features a column-mounted, variable-effort electric  power steering system. It contributes to a 36.7-foot (11.2 meters)  turning diameter, while also saving fuel compared to an engine-driven  power steering pump.</p>  <p><strong> Safety technology helps protect occupants </strong></p> <p>Encore is designed with technologies &ndash; including radar-based  features &ndash; to help avoid crashes and protect occupants in case one  occurs. Technologies that help avoid them include:</p> <ul><li>Standard StabiliTrak electronic stability control</li><li>Four-wheel disc brakes with ABS and electronic brake force distribution</li><li>Cornering brake control and brake assist</li><li>Available all-wheel-drive system that seamlessly shifts power to  the rear wheels when required for better traction and handling</li><li>Available forward collision alert and lane departure warning</li><li>Available front and rear park assist</li><li>Available rear vision camera.</li></ul>  <p>When it comes to occupant protection, the Encore&rsquo;s body structure  forms a protective cage around the passenger compartment. Sixty-three  percent of the underbody structure is comprised of high-strength steels,  while 57 percent of the upper body uses high-strength steels, including  ultra-high-strength steel. That protection is complemented with 10  standard air bags, which include:</p> <ul><li>Driver and front passenger frontal air bags</li><li>Driver and front passenger knee air bags</li><li>Front and rear thorax air bags (all outboard positions)</li><li>Side curtain air bags for front and rear outboard passengers.</li></ul>  <p>Encore&rsquo;s suite of safety features is rounded out with exclusive  OnStar technology, which includes the Advanced Crash Response System,  emergency assistance and more.</p>  <p><strong> Ecotec turbo engine drives efficiency </strong></p> <p>The Buick Encore is powered by an efficient, power-dense Ecotec 1.4L  turbocharged four-cylinder engine and six-speed automatic transmission.  It generates an estimated 140 horsepower (103 kW) and 148 lb.-ft. of  torque (200 Nm) between 1,850 rpm and 4,900 rpm. The wide rpm range for  the maximum torque &ndash; a specific trait of turbocharged engines &ndash; helps it  deliver a better driving experience and performance.</p>  <p>The 1.4L&rsquo;s turbocharger is integrated within the exhaust manifold,  for reduced weight and greater packaging flexibility. A reinforced  crankshaft and forged connecting rods deliver additional strength to  support the engine&rsquo;s pressurized power and torque over a wide rpm band.</p>  <p>Several of the engine&rsquo;s features contribute to the Encore&rsquo;s overall fuel efficiency, including:</p> <ul><li>A high-efficiency alternator</li><li>Regulated voltage control</li><li>Electronically controlled returnless fuel system</li><li>Deceleration fuel cut-off.</li></ul>  <p>They complement other vehicle-wide systems and components that also  help improve efficiency, including low-rolling-resistance tires,  electric power steering, neutral idle feature and optimized  aerodynamics.</p>  <p>A Hydra-Matic 6T40 six-speed automatic transmission backs the Ecotec  1.4L turbo engine. It is part of a family of six-speed front and  all-wheel drive transaxles developed for small cars and crossovers. It  has a unique design, with the planetary gearsets positioned on the same  axis as the engine crankshaft centerline, making the entire powertrain  unit shorter fore-to-aft. This design enables a shorter overall vehicle  length, increased interior space, enhanced safety crush zones and lower  weight.</p>  <p>The Hydra-Matic 6T40 offers a &ldquo;steep&rdquo; first gear and a &ldquo;tall&rdquo;  overdrive top gear to achieve a wide ratio spread of 6.14:1. This allows  the Encore to achieve robust acceleration, as well as fuel efficiency  and lower engine noise at highway cruising speeds. A neutral-gear  disengagement feature helps enhance smoothness and efficiency.</p>  <p>The Buick Encore will begin arriving at U.S. Buick dealerships in the first quarter of 2013.</p><br /><br /></span></p>
