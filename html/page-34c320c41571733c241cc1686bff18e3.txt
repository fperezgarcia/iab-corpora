<SPAN class=Subhead1>Something For Everyone</SPAN><BR>
    Peachtree City and the surrounding areas have a plethora of emerging and enduring cultural groups.&nbsp; From poetry readings to foreign films, look no further than the <A class=Hyperlink title="Peachtree City Library" href="../index.aspx?nid=125" target=_self>Peachtree City Library</A>. The Library hosts monthly book clubs, a thriving poetry group and an international film group. If you are looking for some of the best rising artwork in the area, the library also houses several collections of art throughout the year.
    <P class=Normal><SPAN class=Subhead2>Performing Arts</SPAN><BR>
    If performing arts are more your style, Peachtree City is home to several local theater troupes, dance groups, music performances of all kinds and more. The over 2,000 seat outdoor venue hosts a variety of headliner entertainers from the worlds of pop music, rock &lsquo;n roll, Motown, country and Las Vegas style entertainment.
