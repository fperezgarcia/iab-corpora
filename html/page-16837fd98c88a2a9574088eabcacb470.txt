	<h1 class="who_title">Who is WyoTech?</h1>

	<p>We are not just a tech school.  We're a community of students and instructors who are passionate about the automotive, diesel,<!-- aviation,--> motorcycle, marine, collision &amp; refinishing, plumbing, and electrician career fields.  That's why we're dedicated to supporting our students with superior college-level education in our tech-based training classes. Our career training courses are short-term, and most of our students complete their courses in less than 16 months.<br />
