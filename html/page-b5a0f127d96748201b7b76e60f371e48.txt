				<h2 class="post-title"><a href="http://blog.autowarranties.com/public/item/why-buy-a-sedan" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Why Buy A Sedan?">Why Buy A Sedan?</a></h2>
				<div class="post-info">January 1st, 2009  by Hal Moses</div>
				
				<div class="main">
					<p><a href="http://blog.autowarranties.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/01/images-18.jpg" title="images-18.jpg"><img src="http://blog.autowarranties.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/01/images-18.thumbnail.jpg" alt="images-18.jpg" /></a>What benefits come with purchasing a sedan?  Sedans will give you a balance of performance, handling, fuel-efficiency, safety and easy access.  Also, they handle well in the majority of driving situations.  Unless you are someone who needs to store a lot of cargo, they are a great option.</p>
<p>There are many different choices when looking at sedans.  You could purchase a large, small, family, or luxury sedan.  All of these categories are made up of many models that have different characteristics.  They have various balances of sporty, comfort, and practicality.</p>
<p>Fuel-efficiency varies greatly in different sedans.  Do your homework in this category to make a comparison of different models.</p>
<p>Sedans are equipped with various safety features.  All models that are new have dual front air bags for added safety.  Also standard is three-point seat belts and LATCH systems.  However, certain models also have side air bags and head protection bags.</p>
<p>Do your research to get the sedan that will fit you best.  Also, make sure to buy <a href="http://autowarranties.com/new_car_warranties.htm" title="new car warranties">new car warranties</a> for peace of mind.  Following these simple tips will give you the knowledge to make a good decision when it comes time to purchase a sedan.</p>
