<p>The Nissan LEAF Nismo RC, a competition orientated battery electric vehicle, totally redefines the image of zero emission cars.</p>
<p> <span class="inline inline-left"><img src="http://www.autospectator.com/cars/files/images/2013-Nissan-Leaf-Nismo-RC-001.jpg" alt="2013 Nissan Leaf Nismo RC photo" title="2013 Nissan Leaf Nismo RC photo"  class="image image-_original" width="500" height="333" /><span class="caption" style="width: 498px;"><strong>2013 Nissan Leaf Nismo RC photo</strong></span></span></p>

For the powertrain of this machine, the same high performance lithium ion battery and motor are used as in the world's first mass-produced electric car, the Nissan LEAF. Despite the differences such as the rear doors, rear seat, boot, audio, navigation systems and carpet, you can tell at a glance that this car is related to the Nissan LEAF. However, the similarities between the mass-produced Nissan LEAF and the Nissan LEAF Nismo RC end there. The initials RC, which stand for Racing Competition, this car was developed specially to give a new meaning to the term "Racing Green".</p>
