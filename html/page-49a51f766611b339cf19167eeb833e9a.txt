        <div id="content-area">

<p><center><img src="/sites/www.kaso.com/themes/kaso/images/metal-to-plastic.png" alt="automotive manufacturing" /></center></p>
<p>KASO Plastics has served the Automotive Industry right from our founding in 1962 and has a wide range of experience with injection molded plastics for  use in Industrial Vehicles.  The automation and injection molding equipment at our facility allows us to manufacture close tolerance parts and assemblies that conform to SAE standards and will arrive at your facility ready for final assembly.</p>
<h3>Custom Automotive Injection Molding</h3>
<p>KASO's range of plastics capabilities are ideal for custom automotive plastic <a href="/injection-molding">injection molding</a> and metal-to-plastic part conversion. Production, inventory and <a href="/engineering-services">KASO's engineering services</a> are all located at our facility in Vancouver, Washington.  Our custom automotive plastic injection molding services include helping you to evaluate a variety of engineering grade resins.</p>
<p>Parts requiring close-tolerance specifications with superior quality and aesthetics are no problem for our full service facility.  KASO can also assist with sub assembly and electro-mechanical integration.</p>
