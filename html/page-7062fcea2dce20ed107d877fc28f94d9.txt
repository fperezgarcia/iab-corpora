<p>At Reliable Auto Glass we have been providing all of your auto glass needs, including classic auto glass for over 22 years. From our shop in sunny Redlands, California we provide everything from car windshield replacements to antique auto glass shipped from our office straight to your doorstep. We started out repairing and replacing auto glass on modern cars and found ourselves drawn into the exciting world of classic auto glass through our glass manufacturing connections.</p>
<p>We know that restoring your classic car isn’t just a hobby, it’s a passion. That’s why we stand 100% behind our antique auto glass with full satisfaction guarantee. In the event you aren’t satisfied with your auto glass you may return it for a full refund. Sure, we’re great auto glass specialists, but we’re also focused on superior customer service as well.</p>
<p>Whether you order flat glass such as windows or curved glass for your windshields and back glass you can be assured that your order of classic auto glass will fit your car perfectly, like a key to a lock. At Reliable Auto Glass we use National Auto Glass Specifications (NAGS) OEM patterns to cut the glass for your vintage automobile. These standardized patterns ensure that our glass will perfectly fit your vintage car; all you need to do is provide us with the year, make and model of your car and we can manufacture your glass and ship it to your door. We carry a vast array of flat glass patterns and are expanding our curved glass pattern inventory at a rapid pace.</p>
<p>Even if the unthinkable happens and you and your car end up in a collision and need your car’s glass replaced, Reliable Auto Glass can help. We are listed with several large insurance agencies as approved auto glass specialists and we accept payments by insurance companies to make restoring your prized vehicle less of a hassle.</p>
