<h1>John Sample Automotive Pty Ltd—Moto Guzzi Assorted Motorcycles</h1>
<em>PRA number:</em> 2013/13676<br><em>Date published:</em> 26<sup><small>th</small></sup> July 2013<div class="recallImagesBox"></div>
<h2>Product information</h2>
<h3>Product description</h3>
Please see attached VIN/Model list for all affected Moto Guzzi models<h3>Identifying features</h3>
Please see attached VIN/Model list for all affected Moto Guzzi models<h3>What are the defects?</h3>
A  manufacturing fault has been found with the double connecting rod.<h3>What are the hazards?</h3>
If the double connecting rod fails it will cause the rear of the motorcycle to drop. Under certain circumstance this may cause the rider to fall from the motorcycle.<h3>Where the product was sold</h3>
<ul><li>Nationally</li></ul><h3>Traders who sold this product</h3>
Authorised Moto Guzzi dealers<h3>Supplier</h3>John Sample Automotive Pty Ltd<h2>What should consumers do?</h2>
A letter is being sent to affected owners in order to organise for the replacement part to be fitted free of charge.<h2>Advertisements and supporting documentation</h2>
