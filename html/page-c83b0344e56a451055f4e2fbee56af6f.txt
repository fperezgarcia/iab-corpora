<h1>
    Fine and Performing Arts 
</h1>

<style type="text/css">
    .subnav li:first-child
    {
        margin-left: 0px !important;
    }
    .subnav li
    {
        display: inline !important;

<div id="wide_image" class="wide-img"><image mediaid="{21B06BD0-4574-4FF5-8790-F2309F3AC098}" mediapath="/academics/college-of-arts-sciences/fine-and-performing-arts/fine-and-performing-arts" src="~/media/academics/college-of-arts-sciences/fine-and-performing-arts/fine-and-performing-arts.ashx" /></div>

<div class="intro">
 Fine and Performing Arts at Seattle Pacific University brings together three disciplines: <span><a href="/academics/college-of-arts-sciences/music">Music</a></span>, <a href="http://www.spu.edu/depts/fpa/theatre/tre_homepage.asp">Theatre</a>, and <a href="http://www.spu.edu/depts/viscom/">Art</a>  — each offering you unique challenges and opportunities. They will introduce you to the beauty of the arts and the science of technical artistry while modeling the inherent connections between faith and the arts.
</div>

<div class="col-primary">
    
    <div id="pageBody">
        
		<p>Fine and Performing Arts also provides the SPU community and beyond opportunities for excellent arts experiences, including concerts, theatre performances, and fine art exhibits.</p>

    </div>
    <div class="featureSection"><div class="featureSectCol1"><a href="http://spu.edu/academics/college-of-arts-sciences/music "><image mediaid="{04B7D33E-DF63-40B4-B87D-3D6079EFA92B}" mediapath="/academics/college-of-arts-sciences/fine-and-performing-arts/music" src="~/media/academics/college-of-arts-sciences/fine-and-performing-arts/music.ashx" /></a></div><div class="featureSectCol2"><h3> <a href="http://spu.edu/academics/college-of-arts-sciences/music ">Music Department</a></h3>
		<p>SPU's Music Department offers programs for undergraduates interested in pursuing a career in music, as well as for those wanting to sharpen their musical skills or pursue music as an avocation.</p>
</div><div class="clear"></div></div><div class="featureSection"><div class="featureSectCol1"><a href="http://spu.edu/depts/fpa/theatre/tre_homepage.asp "><image mediaid="{81C0E7FB-6DDB-409A-8CA3-C3396F730BFB}" mediapath="/academics/college-of-arts-sciences/fine-and-performing-arts/theatre" src="~/media/academics/college-of-arts-sciences/fine-and-performing-arts/theatre.ashx" /></a></div><div class="featureSectCol2"><h3> <a href="http://spu.edu/depts/fpa/theatre/tre_homepage.asp ">Theatre Department</a></h3>
		<p>SPU’s Department of Theatre educates, trains, and prepares theatre artists of character and competence who will engage and transform the culture through faithful use of their creative gifts and talents.</p>
</div><div class="clear"></div></div><div class="featureSection"><div class="featureSectCol1"><a href="http://www.spu.edu/depts/viscom/"><image mediaid="{63762DAF-DA16-4654-9F77-1B5D66B85021}" mediapath="/academics/college-of-arts-sciences/fine-and-performing-arts/fine-arts" src="~/media/academics/college-of-arts-sciences/fine-and-performing-arts/fine-arts.ashx" /></a></div><div class="featureSectCol2"><h3> <a href="http://www.spu.edu/depts/viscom/">Art Department</a></h3>
		<p>SPU's Art Department programs include Studio Arts, Visual Communication Design, New Pictures/ Illustration, and Art History, all of which prepare students to begin advanced study or work professionally in art and design.</p>
</div><div class="clear"></div></div>
        
</div>
