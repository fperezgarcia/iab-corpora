	<h2>Where is Fisher Auditorium?</h2>
<p>Fisher is located at the corner of Oakland Ave. and South Eleventh Street on the Indiana University of Pennsylvania campus. The <a title="Map and Directions" href="/WorkArea/linkit.aspx?LinkIdentifier=id&amp;ItemID=1959">maps and directions</a> page has more detailed directions to the IUP campus. Learn more about <a title="Fisher Auditorium" href="/WorkArea/linkit.aspx?LinkIdentifier=id&amp;ItemID=81545">Fisher Auditorium</a>.</p>
<h3>Parking</h3>
<p>The best event parking can be found in the parking garage, directly behind Fisher Auditorium. The garage is located adjacent to Cogswell Hall and can be accessed via Oakland Avenue. The parking garage is open for event parking after 5:00 p.m., and parking is free. The third and fourth floors are open parking; however, there are some reserve spaces on about half of the third floor. Handicap parking is available in the garage. The Washington Street lot (access the street from Fisher Auditorium) is also open to event parking after 5:00 p.m.</p>
