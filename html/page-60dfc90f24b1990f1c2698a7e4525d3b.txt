        	<span class="paragraph-0">
				<p>An intelligent, thought-provoking brand of humor that strays
into nervier, darker territory will be on tap at 8 p.m. Friday as
the Aspen Laff Festival comes to the Center for the Arts, 606 Sixth
St. in Crested Butte.</p>
			</span>
        	<span class="paragraph-1">
				<p>The Aspen Laff Festival showcases three comics who have
distinguished themselves on the club and competition circuit. Tom
Simmons is not afraid to strip long-held beliefs of their
credibility with an unprecedented blend of searing logic and great
jokes. He questions everyone and everything. Winner of the 2009 San
Francisco Comedy Competition, Simmons is also a two-time finalist
at the Boston Comedy Festival and has been seen on Comedy Central,
Showtime and at the Montreal Just for Laughs International Comedy
Festival.</p>
			</span>
        	<span class="paragraph-2">
				<p>Danny Bevins' audacious, irreverent, charring and sarcastic
mouth has gotten him arrested, fined and fired from every job he's
ever had. The son of a Jesus freak and a Green Beret, he straddles
the line between temptation and authority. Bevins is the winner of
the 2000 San Francisco International Comedy Comedy Festival, has
been featured on Comedy Central, Paramount's "The World Stands Up,"
HBO's U.S. Comedy Arts Festival and the Jameson Comedy Tour. His is
a show that crosses boundaries and does not pick sides.</p>
