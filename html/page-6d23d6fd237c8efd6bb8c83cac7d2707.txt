<p>It is controversial origin of the vans, some dating back to the 40 years and even decades before with cars as the unique experimental Dimaxion. The truth is that today&#8217;s concept minivan has a shared origin in the Renault Espace and Chrysler Voyager minivan although the utility already had decades invented, with strange vehicles like the Fiat 500 Giardinera or Mini Clubman original. However, as it is known it today, maybe the Jeep FC-150 had been the real pioneer.</p>
