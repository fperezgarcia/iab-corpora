	<div class="rightLeft-about">
	    <h1>Electric Vehicle Charging Services</h1>
	    <div class="rightLeftText">
        
        <p>There are a lot of reasons to be excited about your electric vehicle (EV)&mdash;including reduced dependence upon fossil fuels and reduced fuel cost. There are also new things to consider including how your EV will affect your electricity bills and how to maximize the environmental benefits of driving your EV.</p>
        
        <p>SolarCity's experts are here to help you assess your EV needs, from a free consultation about energy use to charging station design and installation.</p>
        
        <ul><li><a href="#energy">Energy Use Analysis</a></li>
        <li><a href="#sunshine">Driving on Sunshine</a></li>
        <li><a href="#design">Charging Station Design</a></li></ul>
        
	<h5 id="energy">Energy Use Analysis</h5>
    
    	<p>SolarCity consultants take the time to understand your driving habits and energy bills to help you understand how your electric vehicle use will affect your electricity bills.</p>
        
        <div class="w550">
        <p>We present a customized report detailing your electricity charges as they pertain to your vehicle type, your driving patterns and your local utility's rate structure. </p> 
        
        <p>With a detailed understanding of your energy usage, SolarCity will recommend options for you to maximize your financial savings when you switch to an EV.</p>
        </div>
        
        <div style="width:650px;">
        
        <div style="float:right; padding:20px 0 0 20px;"><p style="color:#6e6e6e;"><img src="/images/ev-gasoline-chart.jpg" /></p></div>
        
	<h5 id="sunshine">Driving on Sunshine</h5>
    
    	<p>A rooftop solar energy system can enable you to drive completely emissions free while dramatically reducing your energy bills.</p>
    	<p>In California, many utilities have special EV energy rates that can help you pile on the savings. SolarCity will help you manage the process of switching rates with your utility.</p>
        </div>
        
  		<div class="w550">
    <h5 id="design">Expert Design for Faster Charging</h5>
  
            <p>SolarCity's consultants can help you understand how to maximize the charging capacity of your existing electrical equipment and what upgrades may be necessary to fit your driving habits and needs.</p>
            
            <p>We install systems that are compatible with most electric vehicles including the Nissan Leaf, Chevy Volt, BMW Active-e and Tesla. We understand and will ensure your system is compatible with electric codes (UL, NEC, etc.) and automotive standards (SAE, etc.).</p>
            
            <p>SolarCity designs and installs electric vehicle  charging systems for homes and businesses. SolarCity has experience with over 2,500 EV charging stations. Our Electric Vehicle Service team members are experts in optimizing charging systems for your electric vehicle or plug-in hybrid model.</p>
            
            <p>We manage every detail of your charging system installation project making sure it is properly designed, permitted and compliant with electric codes and tuned to your specific home and driving habits.</p>
	</div>
