            <div class="primary-article-content"><h4><strong>What is it:</strong></h4><p>Today's Audi A3 is offered as a front- or all-wheel drive five-door hatchback. The all-new <a href="http://www.autotrader.com/audi-a3.jsp">2015 Audi A3</a> sedan, however, uses a more traditional four-door sedan bodystyle. The upcoming A3's US engine lineup has not been announced. But European versions will have three four-cylinder powerplants. Two are gas-powered and make 140hp and 180hp. The last is a 150-hp turbodiesel. While European models will offer manual or automatic transmissions, US buyers will likely only get the automatic. <a href="http://www.autotrader.com/audi-cars.jsp">Audi</a> has not yet confirmed if it plans to keep the hatchback, or only offer the sedan.</p><h4><strong>How much:</strong></h4><p>Pricing is not yet official for the 2015 A3. We expect it to start near the current model's $28,000 base price.</p><h4><strong>When can you get it?</strong></h4><p>First quarter of 2015</p><h4><strong>Add it to your shopping list because...</strong></h4><p>The entry-level luxury segment is heating up, and the 2015 Audi A3 sedan looks like a worthy entrant. The front-wheel drive A3 sedan offers Audi's stylish MMI interface for nearly all controls. It also boasts a bold look, complete with Audi's signature LED headlights. Interior options range from cloth seats to leather and alcantara. That means value-conscious drivers don't have to splurge, while luxury buyers can also be satisfied. And despite its small engines, the A3 is no slouch: 0-to-60 takes 7.3 seconds with the top-level 180hp engine. For drivers who still aren't convinced, a 300-hp S3 sedan is also on the way.</p><h4><strong>Other cars to consider:</strong></h4><p><a href="http://www.autotrader.com/buick-verano.jsp">Buick Verano</a>: Buick's smallest car is a four-door sedan based on the <a href="http://www.autotrader.com/chevrolet-cruze.jsp">Chevrolet Cruze</a>. While it may not boast Audi's brand cachet, it starts at just $24,000. And it's now available with a 250-hp turbo engine, which is more powerful than the A3 or the <a href="http://www.autotrader.com/mercedes+benz-cla_class.jsp">Mercedes CLA.</a></p><p><a href="http://www.autotrader.com/lexus-ct+200h.jsp">Lexus CT 200h</a>: it may not be as sporty as the A3, but the CT 200h is certainly a rival. While it starts around $33,000, it comes very well-equipped. And since it's a hybrid, the CT 200h offers drivers a big savings at the pump.</p><p><a href="http://www.autotrader.com/mercedes+benz-cla_class.jsp">Mercedes Benz CLA</a>: although it isn't out yet, the CLA is the A3's biggest competitor. It's also a small, front-wheel-drive luxury sedan. It also offers a performance variant, the CLA45 AMG. And it, too, will start under $30,000.</p><p><a href="http://www.autotrader.com/audi-a4.jsp">Used Audi A4</a>: if the A3 sedan doesn't offer enough room - or enough zip - there's always the A4. While a new A4 starts above $33,000, used models are much closer to the A3's base price. And like the A3, they offer Audi cachet - and those signature LEDs.</p><p><a href="http://www.autotrader.com/car-news/new-york-auto-show.xhtml">See all New York Auto Show news</a></p></div><div><script type="text/javascript" src="http://tracking.skyword.com/tracker.js?contentId=281474979080516"></script><link rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" href="http://media.autotrader.com/content/layout/article-layout.css" /> <script src='http://cdn.gigya.com/JS/socialize.js?apiKey=2_S9yQ4k4sutW0IemTk38sKxXJ7K9Je1ZPaMFEgQQlyVWGwmHEFUOig3kJt1Qlawfa' type='text/javascript'></script> <script type="text/javascript" src="http://media.autotrader.com/content/layout/article-comments.js"></script></div>
