<h1><strong>FORD</strong> CPO FAQ</h1>
<ul class="faq-menu">
<li data-cid="C1">Certification Process</li>
<li data-cid="C2">Warranties</li>
<li data-cid="C3">Benefits</li>
</ul>
<div class="faq-container">
<div data-cid="C1" class="faq-category-content">
<ul>
<li data-question="Q1">
<div class="faq-question">
<span class="action-button"></span>
<p>Can any Ford vehicle be certified?</p>
</div>
<div class="faq-answer">
No. To become Ford-certified, a pre-owned vehicle has to be less than six years old and have fewer than 80,000 miles on the odometer. It then has to pass a rigorous 172-point inspection. A CARFAX<sup></sup> Vehicle History Report&#153; is also run to identify any vehicle that does not meet our strict standards.
<div class="disclaimer"></div>
</div>
</li>
<li data-question="Q2">
<div class="faq-question">
<span class="action-button"></span>
<p>Who certifies these vehicles?</p>
</div>
<div class="faq-answer">
Ford factory-trained dealer technicians go through each vehicle, performing a rigorous 172-point inspection, including obtaining a CARFAX<sup></sup> Vehicle History Report.&#153; Any part(s) that doesnt meet our standards is replaced with a factory-authorized part. For added peace of mind, each vehicle comes with comprehensive limited warranty coverage and powertrain limited warranty coverage backed by Ford Motor Company.*
<div class="disclaimer">*See your dealer for warranty coverage details.</div>
</div>
</li>
<li data-question="Q3">
<div class="faq-question">
<span class="action-button"></span>
<p>Which areas are inspected during the 172-point inspection for Ford-Certified Pre-Owned vehicles?</p>
</div>
<div class="faq-answer">
<ul class="bullet-list">
<li>Vehicle history</li>
<li>Road test</li>
<li>Exterior/Interior appearance and condition</li>
<li>Diagnostics</li>
<li>Underhood/Underbody components</li>
<li>Hybrid system</li>
<li>Convenience features</li>
</ul>
<div class="disclaimer"></div>
</div>
</li>
<li data-question="Q4">
<div class="faq-question">
<span class="action-button"></span>
<p>Why is a CARFAX<sup></sup> Vehicle History Report&#153; so important? </p>
</div>
<div class="faq-answer">
The CARFAX Vehicle History Report can identify identify any vehicle that does not meet our strict standards. The report is part of our 172-point inspection process, and it includes checks for:<br /><br />
<ul class="bullet-list">
<li>Flood vehicles</li>
<li>Fire damage</li>
<li>Frame damage</li>
<li>Salvage vehicles</li>
<li>Branded title</li>
<li>Odometer issues</li>
<li>Accident history</li>
<li>Vehicle title history</li>
<ul>
<div class="disclaimer"></div>
</div>
</li>
<li data-question="Q5">
<div class="faq-question">
<span class="action-button"></span>
<p>What happens when an item does not pass during the 172-point inspection?</p>
</div>
<div class="faq-answer">
Any item(s) that does not meet Ford standards is repaired or replaced with a factory-authorized part(s).
<div class="disclaimer"></div>
</div>
</li>
<li data-question="Q6">
<div class="faq-question">
<span class="action-button"></span>
<p>Can I get a Ford-Certified Pre-Owned vehicle from a non-Ford dealer?</p>
</div>
<div class="faq-answer">
No. Only a Ford Motor Company-franchised Dealer can sell Ford-Certified Pre-Owned vehicles that were inspected and approved by Ford Motor Company standards.
<div class="disclaimer"></div>
</div>
</li>
</ul>
</diV>
<div data-cid="C2" class="faq-category-content">
<ul>
<li data-question="Q7">
<div class="faq-question">
<span class="action-button"></span>
<p>Do Ford-Certified Pre-Owned vehicles come with warranty coverage?</p>
</div>
<div class="faq-answer">
Yes, every Ford Certified Pre-Owned vehicle comes with a comprehensive limited warranty backed by Ford Motor Company covering more than 500 components for 12 months/12,000 miles* plus seven-year/100,000-mile powertrain limited warranty coverage.
<div class="disclaimer">*See your dealer for warranty details.</div>
</div>
</li>
<li data-question="Q8">
<div class="faq-question">
<span class="action-button"></span>
<p>What areas are included in the comprehensive limited warranty?</p>
</div>
<div class="faq-answer">
<ul class="bullet-list">
<li>Engine</li>
<li>Transmission</li>
<li>Rear-wheel drive system</li>
<li>Front-wheel drive system</li>
<li>Steering components</li>
<li>Brakes</li>
<li>Front and rear suspension</li>
<li>Electrical system</li>
<li>Air conditioning and heating</li>
<li>Technology components</li>
<li>Emissions components</li>
<li>Factory audio system</li>
<li>Safety systems</li>
</ul>
<div class="disclaimer"></div>
</div>
</li>
<li data-question="Q8">
<div class="faq-question">
<span class="action-button"></span>
<p>What's included in the powertrain warranty?</p>
</div>
<div class="faq-answer">
Areas of coverage include the engine, transmission and more.
<div class="disclaimer"></div>
</div>
</li>
<li data-question="Q10">
<div class="faq-question">
<span class="action-button"></span>
<p>If I sell my Ford-Certified Pre-Owned vehicle and the buyer wants to transfer warranty coverage, how much will it cost?</p>
</div>
<div class="faq-answer">
It wont cost the buyer a penny. Our manufacturer-backed limited warranty is fully transferable to future owners at no cost.
<div class="disclaimer"></div>
</div>
</li>
</ul>
</diV>
<div data-cid="C3" class="faq-category-content">
<ul>
<li data-question="Q11">
<div class="faq-question">
<span class="action-button"></span>
<p>Why should I buy a Ford-Certified Pre-Owned vehicle?</p>
</div>
<div class="faq-answer">
Ford Certified Pre-Owned (CPO) vehicles go through a rigorous172-point inspection process. Any item(s) that does not pass is replaced or repaired with a factory-authorized part. Something else you get with a Ford Certified Pre-Owned vehicle  warranty coverage. This includes comprehensive limited warranty coverage for more than 500 components for 12 months/12,000 miles* and fully transferable powertrain limited warranty coverage for seven years/100,000 miles.* Another CPO benefit you wont get with a used vehicle is complimentary 24-Hour Roadside Assistance available seven days a week, 365 days a year.
<div class="disclaimer">*See your dealer for warranty details.</div>
</div>
</li>
<li data-question="Q12">
<div class="faq-question">
<span class="action-button"></span>
<p>What happens if my Ford-Certified Pre-Owned vehicle gets a flat tire or I need a battery jump? </p>
</div>
<div class="faq-answer">
Another perk to a Ford-Certified Pre-Owned vehicle vs. used  complimentary roadside assistance. Just call us and well change the tire or jump the battery at no cost to you. Other services include:<br /><br />
<ul class="bullet-list">
<li>Towing assistance of up to $100 for warranty and non-warranty problems, including accidents (excludes impound and repossession)</li>
<li>Travel expense reimbursement of up to $500, for up to three days lodging, meals and rental vehicle for travel expenses accrued when the breakdown occurs more than 100 miles from home</li>
<li>Destination expense assistance of up to $75 for emergency transportation to the immediate destination</li>
<li>Rental car reimbursement of up to $30 per day for up to five days</li>
<li>Fuel delivery if the vehicle runs out of fuel  up to two gallons at no charge</li>
<li>Lockout assistance of up to $50 to unlock vehicle doors</li>
<li>Service throughout the 50 United States and Canada</li>
</ul>
<div class="disclaimer"></div>
</div>
</li>
<li data-question="Q13">
<div class="faq-question">
<span class="action-button"></span>
<p>Can I get Ford Credit financing on a Ford-Certified Pre-Owned vehicle?</p>
</div>
<div class="faq-answer">
Absolutely. In fact, sometimes <a href="/certified-used/incentives/" target"_blank">special financing</a> is available.
<div class="disclaimer"></div>
</div>
</li>
<li data-question="Q14">
<div class="faq-question">
<span class="action-button"></span>
<p>How can I find out what Ford-Certified Pre-Owned vehicles are available at my local Ford dealer?</p>
</div>
<div class="faq-answer">
Simply do an <a href="/certified-used/search/" target"_blank">inventory search</a>.
<div class="disclaimer"></div>
