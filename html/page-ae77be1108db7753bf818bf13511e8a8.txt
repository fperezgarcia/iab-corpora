FAQ
<p>
Q: I want to sell my car fast. How can I sell my car,  at this web site.
</p>
<p>
A: You can sell your car by listing it at used-cars.info web site.
First create a user account (register/sign up), and then login anytime to post auto ads (US, UK, Canada only).
</p>
<p>
Q: Can I sell my truck too.
</p>
<p>
A: Yes, any type of vehicle for sale or lease in US, Canada or UK can be posted. If your truck is not a pickup truck, such as a commercial truck or dump truck, etc. choose "other truck" from the menu, when you create your truck  ad.
</p>
<p>
Q: I'm an auto dealer, not a private seller. I have pre-owned vehicles and new cars for sale. Can I post ads here?
</p>
<p>
A:Yes, it doesn't matter whether you are a private vehicle seller, or a car dealer  or whether you sell used cars or sell brand new cars and vehicles. You can list your auto dealership business too.
</p>
<p>
Q: How about cycles, snow vehicles, aircrafts, watercrafts. Can I sell my boat?
</p>
<p>
A: Yes, the term vehicle refers to any kind of vehicle. You can list your  boat,  canoe, yacht, bike, motorcycle, tricycle, plane,  helicopter, snowmobile, ATV, trailer, bus, motorhome, rv, van. Even a spacecraft can be posted. 
</p>
<p>
Q: I want to buy an affordable reliable used car. What can I do?
</p>
<p>
A: You can search or browse auto listings by location, or create a vehicle wanted ad.
</p>
<p>
Q: Can we advertise our auto service. Is there any cost?
</p>
<p>
A: Yes. You can advertise your auto business. All listings are posted for free, and there is no hidden or future costs. Car insurance services, tire stores, car repairs, gas stations, driving lessons, auto shows, dui attorneys, etc can be posted. 
</p>
<p>
Q: Can I trust buyers/sellers when I am trying to buy a car or sell my car at this place.
</p>
<p>
A: We can't control who responds to your auto ads. We make efforts 
to remove suspicious vehicle listings, but there might be 
fake ads that we can't catch. Most info on a car ad including the vehicle identification number (VIN) can be correct, and still the person who posted the listing may have bad intentions.
</p>
<p>
Q: I have 2001 Honda Accord automatic sedan car in good condition. How much should I ask for it?
</p>
<p>
A: You determine the price. Used-cars.info doesn't appraise, buy or sell cars. We don't give any advice. You may want to check car values at other sites like Kelley Blue Book, Nada, Edmunds.</p>
<p>
Q: I can't find cars near my place.
</p>
<p>
A: You can check this web site at another time, or place a car wanted ad. 
</p>
<p>
Q: When do classified ads expire at used-cars.info ?
</p>
<p>
A: No official expiration date. You can remove your ad anytime, or ask us to do it if you don't remember your login info.
</p>
<p>
Q: I want to sell my car fast. How long does it take for me to find a serious buyer if I advertise my car here.
</p>
<p>
A: It depends mostly on site traffic (currently thousands of unique visitors per day, and growing), and  price and condition of your car. If you have a web page, you can link to some pages of this site, and this can increase your and other people's chances of finding vehicles and  buyers.
directly or indirectly.
</p>

Q: I see almost ten thousands ads here, but you have low traffic and you are a new web site. How did you get so many ads in such a short period time.
</p>

<p>

A: Good question. All ads are original, meaning they are not taken from elsewhere. People were posting ads and getting free leads. This web site was growing gradually. Suddenly it was penalized for an unknown reason and there is no explanation. Probably some search engines were tricked by spammers who steal content and post at various places etc. But things should get back to normal soon.
</p>
