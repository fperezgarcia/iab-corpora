													<h1>Entertainment Arts</h1>
								<div class="tagline"></div>
			</div>
			
			<div class="repeatingsection"></div>
								
		</div>
		
		<div id="diagonalrepeat">
					</div>
		
	
		<div class="page">
			<div id="main" class="entertainment-arts">	<!-- MAIN -->

				<div> <!-- MAIN CONTENT DIV -->
					<div class="pod regular REVIEW">
<p>from TDI and <a href="http://www.collegeforcreativestudies.edu/hs/academics/entertainment_arts" title="http://www.collegeforcreativestudies.edu/hs/academics/entertainment_arts" target="_blank">http://www.collegeforcreativestudies.edu/hs/academics/entertainment_arts</a></p>
</div><div class="pod regular">
<p><span class="asset alignCenter" style='width: 1200px;'><img src='http://d3ub7tefq1g6wg.cloudfront.net/includes/files/assets/images/large/1344284460_entertainment_arts.jpg' alt='Entertainment Arts Hero' width='1200' height='399.5' /></span></p>
</div><div class="pod columns">
<div class="pod column first" style='width:75%;margin-right:0%;'>
<div class="pod regular PADDED">
<p>Storytelling is at the heart of your role as an artist, whether you&rsquo;re venturing into animation, video or games. Our program prepares you to join the next generation of storytellers while teaching you the latest software used in the entertainment industry.</p>
<p>Choose from three concentrations:</p>
<div class="pod columns">
<div class="pod column first" style='width:31.68%;margin-right:2%;'>
<div class="pod regular DIVIDERDARK">
<h2>Animation</h2>
</div><div class="pod regular">
<p>Animation introduces you to the art of sequential storytelling and utilizes stop-motion, material-based and computer-generated (CG) imagery techniques.</p>
</div></div><div class="pod column" style='width:31.68%;margin-right:2%;'>
<div class="pod regular DIVIDERDARK">
<h2>Game</h2>
</div><div class="pod regular">
<p>Game concentration teaches you how to produce 2-D and 3-D images and concepts for games.</p>
</div></div><div class="pod column last" style='width:32.64%;'>
<div class="pod regular DIVIDERDARK">
<h2>Video</h2>
</div><div class="pod regular">
<p>Video can help you learn how to use video as an exploratory and expressive medium through narrative, experimental and documentary forms.<b></b></p>
</div></div><div class='clearBoth'></div></div><div class="pod regular">
<h2>What Sets Us Apart?</h2>
<p><b>Direct Contacts to Industry</b><br />It&rsquo;s all about who you know, especially in Hollywood. Whether you want to work for Pixar, do CG graphics for major motion pictures, design the next character for a Rock Star game or direct the next Oscar-winning production, CCS students have to have direct ties to industry by way of our alumni. Within CCS&rsquo; Entertainment Arts department, you will be taught by faculty who have worked on films such as Wall-E, designed for major video games and have been a part of major Hollywood productions.<br /><b></b></p>
<p><b>Customizable Curriculum</b><br />All fields within the entertainment industry require different talents and skill sets. At CCS, you will have the opportunity to customize your curriculum to your liking. Do you want to learn how to animate with a strong focus on high-end computer-generated images? Are you looking to design video games with a focus on character development? Here, you&rsquo;ll have the chance to create your own pathway.<br /><b></b></p>
<p><b>Focus on Concept</b><br />The benefit of studying Entertainment Arts at an art and design college is the focus on the creative process and the concept. At CCS, our process first starts with the creative and then moves on to character performance and emotion.<br /><b></b></p>
<p><b>Industry-Grade Equipment</b><br />We&rsquo;ve invested in top-of-the-line equipment, knowing that it&rsquo;s vital that students learn on the same equipment that they will eventually use in studios around the world.</p>
</div></div></div><div class="pod column last" style='width:25%;'>
