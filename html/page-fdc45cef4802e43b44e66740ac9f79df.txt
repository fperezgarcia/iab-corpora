        <div class="fb-like" data-href="http://www.examiner.com/article/2010-chicago-auto-show-best-wagon" data-layout="button_count" data-send="false" data-show-faces="false" data-width="90"></div>        
  <div property="schema:articleBody content:encoded" class="article__body">
    <p>
	My best station wagon of the Detroit Auto Show was the Subaru Impreza STi, and while Subaru even featured a police car version of their high performance wagon I had to pick Subbie&rsquo;s rival for the best wagon of the <a href="/topic/2010-chicago-auto-show/articles" class="inline_link">2010 Chicago Auto Show</a>. The <a href="/topic/mitsubishi" class="inline_link">Mitsubishi</a> Lancer RalliArt is somewhat of a sportback (this is the word they use to avoid using &ldquo;station wagon&rdquo;) version of the popular Lancer Evolution line and it offers a 237 horsepower turbocharged 4-cylinder mated to a dual clutch automatic transmission and an intricate all wheel drive system. </p>
<p>&#13;</p>
<p>&#13;<br />
	I think that this lower performance version of the Lancer provides a great alternative to those who don&rsquo;t really NEED the Evo X&rsquo;s power and the sportback model should be fine competition for the Impreza wagon. Sadly, you can&rsquo;t get the full Evo drivetrain in the sportback frame but the RalliArt models are a great cost effective option to the Evolution X and that is why it is my best wagon of the 2010 Chicago Auto Show.</p>
<p>&#13;</p>
<p>&#13;<br />
	Stay tuned to the Detroit Autos Examiner as I bring you the best and worst of the 2010 Chicago Auto Show plus live coverage of the 2010 Detroit Autorama!</p>
