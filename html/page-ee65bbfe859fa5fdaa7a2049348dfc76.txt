  <div class="content clear-block">
    <p>Champaign County&#39;s affordability and commitment to the arts have made it home to a large number of artists, galleries festivals and more.&nbsp; From first run Indie films to big name bands and sketch improv to a progressive countywide arts festival, Champaign County has impressive performing arts facilities and galleries that attract top notch entertainment options sure to delight all ages.</p>

<div id="spotlight" class="cvb-spotlight"> <h2>Spotlight</h2></div>
<div id="spotlight-sep" class="cvb-spotlight-sep"></div> 
  <div class="content"><div class="view view-Spotlight view-id-Spotlight view-display-id-block_1 view-dom-id-3">
    
  
  
      <div class="view-content">
        <div class="views-row views-row-1 views-row-odd views-row-first">
    <div id="node-1457" class="cvb-spotlight-content">
	<div style="clear: both; width: 400px; padding-bottom: 5px;">
    		<h3>Krannert Center for the Performing Arts</h3>
	    </div>
  <div class="cvb-spotlight-content-thumb"><img src="/sites/default/files/ArtsEntertainment-Krannert.jpg" align="left" /></div>
  <div id="cvb-spotlight-content-content" class="cvb-spotlight-content-content">
  	<p>What Lincoln Center is to New York and Kennedy Center is to Washington D.C., <a href="http://krannertcenter.com/">Krannert Center</a> is to the Midwest - a magnificent showcase for the performing arts.&nbsp; This professional laboratory for the University of Illinois dance, theatre, opera and music departments hosts internationally famous artists such as Ravi Shankar, Emmy Lou Harris, Herbie Hancock, the Chicago Symphony Orchestra and Mark Morris Dance Group.&nbsp; The Promenade gift shop, Intermezzo cafe, and Ticket Office can be found in the vast lobby connecting the four main theatres.
</p>  </div>
</div>
  </div>
  <div class="views-row views-row-2 views-row-even">
    <div id="node-1458" class="cvb-spotlight-content">
	<div style="clear: both; width: 400px; padding-bottom: 5px;">
    		<h3>Champaign-Urbana Symphony Orchestra</h3>
	    </div>
  <div class="cvb-spotlight-content-thumb"><img src="/sites/default/files/CUSO_Spotlight_0.jpg" align="left" /></div>
  <div id="cvb-spotlight-content-content" class="cvb-spotlight-content-content">
  	<p>Today, the <a href="http://www.cusymphony.org/">Champaign-Urbana Symphony</a> is designated as the &ldquo;Professional Orchestra in Residence at the Krannert Center for the Performing Arts at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.&rdquo; The mission of the Symphony is to enhance the quality of life in the community by providing symphonic music of the highest caliber through live performances and music education in the schools, the concert hall and the community.</p>
  </div>
</div>
  </div>
  <div class="views-row views-row-3 views-row-odd views-row-last">
    <div id="node-1923" class="cvb-spotlight-content">
	<div style="clear: both; width: 400px; padding-bottom: 5px;">
    		<h3>40 North | 88 West</h3>
	    </div>
  <div class="cvb-spotlight-content-thumb"><img src="/sites/default/files/40north-logo.gif" align="left" /></div>
  <div id="cvb-spotlight-content-content" class="cvb-spotlight-content-content">
  	<p><a href="http://www.40north.org//">40 North </a>is dedicated to fueling the growth and well-being of Champaign County by nurturing its arts, culture and entertainment community. Visitors enjoy numberous innovative programs including Art &amp; Sol, Sight Specific and the annual Boneyard Arts Festival.</p>
