                        <td rowspan="2" align="left" valign="top" class="bodycopy"><h1>Roadside Rescuer: Towing and labor services</h1>
                          <p><em>Roadside  Rescuer</em>,  SECURA’s towing and labor services, minimizes the frustration and inconvenience  of being stranded on the road. Feel confident knowing that you have the help  you need, no matter where or when. </p>
                          <p>Even when  you’re traveling in vehicles you don’t own, you can use <em>Roadside Rescuer’s</em> services.</p>
                          <h2><em>Roadside Rescuer</em> services</h2>
                          <ul>
                            <li>Tire changes </li>
                            <li>Emergency fluid  delivery </li>
                            <li>Dead battery  jumpstarts </li>
                            <li>Lockout service </li>
                            <li>Minor mechanical first aid if you have an  accident, stalled vehicle, or are stuck in mud or snow</li>
                            <li>Towing an auto to the nearest qualified repair  facility or up to 10 miles to the repair facility of your choice</li>
                            <li>Towing trailers with towing and labor coverage</li>
                            </ul>
                          <h2><em>Roadside Rescuer</em> features</h2>
                          <ul>
                            <li>Coverage for the  policyholder and immediate family members in autos they own that have towing  coverage</li>
                            <li>Extra coverage for the  named insured and additional named insured when they are traveling in  automobiles they do not own</li>
                            <li>Arranges for alternative  transportation</li>
                            <li>Contacts your home or  office to report that you are stranded or your auto has broken down</li>
                            <li>Other reasonable services  to ease the inconvenience of the roadside emergency</li>
                            <li>Sign-and-drive coverage,  which does not require you to pay up front and wait for reimbursement</li>
                            </ul>
                          <p><em>Roadside Rescuer</em> is included at no additional premium on  MILE-STONE® <em>Gold</em> policy vehicles that  have physical damage coverage. MILE-STONE <em>Basic</em> and auto policies, and trailers with towing and labor coverage also receive <em>Roadside Rescuer</em>.</p>
