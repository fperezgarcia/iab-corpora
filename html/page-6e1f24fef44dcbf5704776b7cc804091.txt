                    <h1>Arts and entertainment </h1>
        
                    
                    
                    
                    
        <p>Alberta has a vibrant arts and entertainment scene.</p>
        <h2>What is happening in my community?</h2>
        <p>One of the best ways to find out what is happening in your community is to look at local entertainment or community newspapers which will list a range of free and paid events, festivals, theatre productions, museum and art exhibitions, and more.</p>
        <p>Links for entertainment or community weekly newspapers and magazines for Alberta’s major centre are shown below. Visit the <a target="_blank"  href="http://www.awna.com/awna-member-listing">Alberta Weekly Newspaper Association</a> site to find community newspapers for smaller centres. </p>
        <h3>Airdrie</h3>
        <p><a target="_blank"  href="http://www.airdriecityview.com/">Airdrie City View</a></p>
        <p><a target="_blank"  href="http://www.airdrieecho.com/">Airdrie Echo</a></p>
        <h3>Calgary</h3>
        <p><a target="_blank"  href="http://www.ffwdweekly.com/">Fastforward Weekly</a></p>
        <p><a target="_blank"  href="http://swervecalgary.com/">Swerve</a></p>
        <h3>Edmonton</h3>
        <p><a target="_blank"  href="http://www.vueweekly.com/">Vue Weekly</a></p>
        <h3>Fort McMurray</h3>
        <p><a target="_blank"  href="http://www.fortmcmurraytoday.com/">Fort McMurray Today</a></p>
        <h3>Grand Prairie</h3>
        <p><a target="_blank"  href="http://www.dailyheraldtribune.com/">Daily Herald-Tribune</a></p>
        <h3>Lethbridge</h3>
        <p><a target="_blank"  href="http://www.lethbridgeliving.com/">Lethbridge Living</a></p>
        <p><a target="_blank"  href="http://www.lethbridgejournal.com">The Lethbridge Journal</a></p>
        <h3>Medicine Hat</h3>
        <p><a target="_blank"  href="http://www.medicinehatnews.com/">Medicine Hat News</a></p>
        <h3>Red Deer</h3>
        <p><a target="_blank"  href="http://www.albertalocalnews.com/reddeerexpress/">Red Deer Express</a></p>
        <p><a target="_blank"  href="http://www.albertalocalnews.com/reddeeradvocate/">Red Deer Advocate</a></p>
        <h3>Sherwood Park</h3>
        <p><a target="_blank"  href="http://www.sherwoodparknews.com/">Sherwood Park - Strathcona County News</a></p>
        <h3>St. Albert</h3>
        <p><a target="_blank"  href="http://www.stalbertgazette.com/">St. Albert Gazette</a></p>

        <p>Alberta Culture and Community Spirit publishes a <a target="_blank"  href="http://culture.alberta.ca/events/default.aspx">cultural calendar</a> to promote community arts, heritage, volunteer and other cultural information and activities.</p>
