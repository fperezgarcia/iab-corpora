<p><i>Answer by D B</i><br/>There are a few different forms.</p>
<p>Rally Racing .  Set up in stages which could be either pavement or dirt<br />
Rally Cross.     Set up so there is both pavement and dirt during a stage or lap.<br />
Auto Cross. ( Solo )   Set up in large parking lots with a coned off route. Sometimes against 1 other car but mostly alone against the clock. Also there is a type where they shut down a section of a road  and you race against an index speed.<br />
Off Road.    Set up to race all off road where there may or may not be a road.<br />
Land Speed Racing.  Set up on a dry lake bed either dry mud or salt . A straight line course to set new speed records.</p>
