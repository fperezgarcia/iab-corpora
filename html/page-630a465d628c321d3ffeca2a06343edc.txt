<h1>Lincoln College of Technology</h1>

<p>The Lincoln College of Technology, formerly Nashville Auto-Diesel College, campus is located at 1524 Gallatin Road in Nashville, Tennessee. With an impressive history in automotive training, Lincoln College of Technology features a fully-equipped auto shop facility, where students will get hands-on training from ASE Certified Instructors. Students can choose from a variety of diesel, collision repair and high performance training programs, including an ASE Master Automotive Program and Custom Paints Program. Lincoln College of Technology also offers on-campus dormitories and knowledgeable Housing Assistance, making it an ideal destination school for relocation.</p>

<p>Lincoln College of Technology is a member of the Lincoln Group of Schools with campuses nationwide. Training programs are designed to provide students with skills that are marketable today and with knowledge that will allow them to grow and realize their maximum career potential in the future. Lincoln College of Technology is committed to the advancement of career education, and dedicated to individuals who desire to improve themselves and their opportunity for career success.</p>

<p><strong>Accreditation:</strong><br/>
Lincoln College of Technology, formerly known as Nashville Auto-Diesel College, is accredited by the Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges (ACCSC), and authorized for operation as a post-secondary educational institution by the Tennessee Higher Education Commission.</p>
