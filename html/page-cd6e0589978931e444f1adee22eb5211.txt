    <h1>Braun Entervan Dodge/Chrysler and Honda Minivans</h1>
    <img src="images_vans/2011-chrysler-dodge-entervan.png" alt="MobilityWorks Dodge-Chrysler Entervan" align="right">
    <h2>BraunAbility Dodge-Chrysler Entervan &amp; XT Standard Features</h2>
<p>    The Entervan Conversion is available on the Dodge Grand Caravan, Chrysler Town & County and Now the Honda Odyssey minivan. The Entervan-XT Conversion for wheelchair vans extends the accessible features of the standard Entervan to new heights with 56-1/2" of clearance through the sliding door - a full 4" more than the standard Entervan model. The XT also provides up to 61" of interior headroom clearance.</p>
    
      <p><span class="detail_header">Auto Door</span> The OEM door operator reliably opens the sliding door at the touch of a button. This feature can be activated with the standard keychain remote, or with one of the five interior control switches.</p>
   
    <p><span class="detail_header">Auto Kneel</span> To make entering and exiting the vehicle even easier, the Braun Entervan features an integrated "kneeling" system. An electric actuator lowers the rear suspension while the door opens, which reduces the slope of the automatic ramp.</p>
    
    <p><span class="detail_header">Auto Ramp</span> The lightweight aluminum ramp extends to allow easy access to the interior for wheelchairs and scooters. In the event of power failure, both the door and ramp can easily be operated manually in the Braun Entervan.</p>
    
    <p><span class="detail_header">Lowered Floor</span> From the toe pan to the rear axle the Braun Entervan's floor is up to 16" lower than conventional minivans. With a choice of seating positions, wheelchair users can choose to ride in their chair and enjoy the view.</p>
    
    <p><span class="detail_header">Removable Seating</span> For those who want to ride up front or even drive from their wheelchair, the Braun Entervan makes it easy: simply unlock the seat bases and roll the entire seat out of the vehicle. The seats can be reinstalled just as easily when needed.</p>
