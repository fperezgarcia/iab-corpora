			<h1>Play <span style="color: #000000;">|</span> <em>Arts</em> &amp; <em>Entertainment</em></h1>
<p>Overview | <a href="museums/">Museums</a> | <a href="theaters/">Theatres</a> | <a href="night-life/">Night Life</a></p>
<h4>Overview</h4>
<p>The arts are alive and well in the Iowa Lakes Corridor. There are many local artists, studios and galleries throughout our region along with any number of performing artists. We are home to community theaters and the <a href="http://www.stephens.edu/news/stephensevents/okoboji/" target="_blank">Okoboji Summer Theatre</a>. Many of our local artists participate in the <a href="http://www.artisansroadtrip.com/" target="_blank">Artisans Road Trip</a> (ART) held in October of each year. Check out the <a href="theaters/">theatres</a> and <a href="night-life/">night life</a> sections to find things to do.</p>
<p><strong> </strong></p>
<div id="attachment_1241" class="wp-caption alignleft" style="width: 230px"><strong><strong><a rel="attachment wp-att-1241" href="http://www.lakescorridor.com/play/arts-and-entertainment/myers-pottery-1/"><img class="size-medium wp-image-1241" title="Myers pottery" src="http://www.lakescorridor.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/08/Myers-pottery-1-220x165.jpg" alt="" width="220" height="165" /></a></strong></strong><p class="wp-caption-text">Pottery made by local artist Mel Myers.</p></div>
<p><br class="spacer_" /></p>
<p><br class="spacer_" /></p>
<p><strong>ArtsLIVE</strong> is a non-profit partnership of art venues, organizations, and individuals promoting the arts year round in and beyond the Creative Corridor in Northwest Iowa. The vision of ArtsLIVE is that one day, through the collaborative efforts of ArtsLIVE partners, the Creative Corridor will become a year round arts destination.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.artslive.com/" target="_blank"><img class="alignright size-full wp-image-642" title="Arts Live" src="http://www.lakescorridor.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/08/arts-live.gif" alt="Arts Live" width="284" height="122" /></a></p>
<p>ArtsLIVE partners believe in working together, expanding beyond traditional borders to new partners, and improving quality of life through arts, entertainment, and cultural opportunities. For a calendar of art and cultural events in the Corridor, visit <a href="http://www.artslive.com/" target="_blank">http://www.artslive.com/</a>.</p>
<h4 style="clear: left;">Art Centers</h4>
<p>Our local art centers host quality and a diverse array of exhibits each month, featuring national, regional and local artists. Each offers classes for the novice and experienced artist alike. Check out their gift galleries and special events scheduled throughout the year.
