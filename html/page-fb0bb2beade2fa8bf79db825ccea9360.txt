<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://autoroadsidea.blogspot.com/2013/01/transfer-window-deadline-day-evening.html'>Transfer window deadline day: evening updates</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-2911192043977777286' itemprop='description articleBody'>
The race is on to complete last-minute signings and against-the-clock deals, as the transfer window will close in less than 11 hours.<br />Rumours are abound and there has been a flurry of activity this morning, with a number of deals being finalised. <br />We'll keep you up-to-date with the latest news and gossip until the window closes, with a morning, afternoon and evening round-up.<br /><br /><iframe src='http://embed.scribblelive.com/Embed/v5.aspx?Id=81120&ThemeId=9328' width='555' height='370' frameborder='0' style='border: 1px solid #000'></iframe>  To read this morning's transfer news, <a href="http://www.thisisbristol.co.uk/Transfer-window-deadline-day-morning-updates/story-18012710-detail/story.html" target=new rel=nofollow class=llblue>click here</a>.  <b> UPDATE 5.45PM: Birmingham City </B> have secured the signature of <b> Bournemouth </b> striker Wes Thomas, Sky Sports understands.  The club have pipped to the post Championship rivals <b> Blackpool </b> and <b>Crystal Palace</b>.  The loan move is with a view to a permanent switch.  <b> UPDATE 5.40PM: Hull City </b> have officially signed Gedo and Fathi on loan from <b>Al-Ahly</b>:  <img src="http://www.thisisbristol.co.uk/images/localpeople/ugc-images/275775/binaries/Hull%20tweet.JPG"width="595">  The pair will remain at the club until the end of the season. Both deals are subject to international clearance, the club said.  <b> UPDATE 5.35PM: Manchester United's </B> Davide Petrucci has extended his <B> Peterborough United </B> loan spell until the end of the season.   <b> UPDATE 5.30PM: Swindon </B> are closing in on the signing of Bradley Wright-Phillips from <B> Charlton </B>, Sky Sports understands.   <b> UPDATE 5.30PM: Tottenham </B> have confirmed goalkeeper Heurelho Gomes will spend the rest of the season on loan at <B>Hoffenheim</B>.  <b> UPDATE 5.10PM: Dundee FC </B> have signed David Morgan on loan until the end of the season from <b> Nottingham Forest </b>.  <B> UPDATE 5.10PM: Coventry City </B> defender Aaron Phillips has joined <B> Nuneaton Town </B> on a one-month loan deal.  <B> UPDATE 5.05PM: </B> David Beckham has confirmed during his press conference at <b> Paris St Germain </b> he will donate his entire five-month salary to a French children's charity.  <script src="//storify.com/EMcFarnon/january-transfer-window-deadline-day-your-views-1.js?template=slideshow"></script><noscript>[<a href="//storify.com/EMcFarnon/january-transfer-window-deadline-day-your-views-1" target="_blank">View the story "January transfer window deadline day: your views" on Storify</a>]</noscript>  <b> UPDATE 4.45PM: </B> David Beckham has joined the Ligue 1 club <B> Paris St Germain </B> on a five-month deal.  <b> UPDATE 4.10PM: Crewe </b> have re-signed <b> Arsenal </b> midfielder Chuks Aneke on loan:  <img src="http://www.thisisbristol.co.uk/images/localpeople/ugc-images/275775/binaries/Crewe%20resign.JPG"width="595">   <b> UPDATE 3.55PM: Stoke </b> have given the go-ahead for Rory Delap to join npower Championship club <b> Barnsley </b> on loan until the end of the season.  Manager Tony Pulis told <a href="http://www.stokecityfc.com/news/article/delap-barnsley-move-630790.aspx" target=new rel=nofollow class=llblue>the club's official website</a>: "Rory has been a bedrock of the progress this Club has made since he joined us.   "It's important therefore that we look after those players who have served us so well and helped us to get where we are today.  "One way or another, it's been a frustrating time for him this season, but we are delighted that he's now got the chance to go out and play regular first team football.   "I am sure that he will prove to be a great acquisition for Barnsley."  <b> UPDATE 3.55PM: QPR's </b> Alejandro Faurlin has joined Italian outfit <b> Palermo </b> on loan, the club has confirmed on its Twitter page.   <b> UPDATE 3.50PM: </B>  <img src="http://www.thisisbristol.co.uk/images/localpeople/ugc-images/275775/binaries/Stoke%20announcement.jpg"width="595">  <b> UPDATE 3.15PM: </B> Alexander Doni has been released by <B> Liverpool </B> with immediate effect.  <B> UPDATE 3.15PM: Barnsley </B> have terminated the contracts of striker Mido and defender Lee Collins by mutual consent.  <b> UPDATE 3PM: Stoke </B> have agreed a deal with <B> Birmingham </B> for goalkeeper Jack Butland, Sky Sports reports.   <b> UPDATE 2.35PM: Crystal Palace </B> are keen to make a deadline day move for <B> Blackpool </B> striker Kevin Phillips, Sky Sports understands.   <b> UPDATE 2.30PM: Crewe </B> have extended midfielder Bradden Inman's loan from <B> Newcastle </B> until the end of the season.  <b> UPDATE 2.25PM: Swansea </b> have tweeted about the departure of Danny Graham:   <img src="http://www.thisisbristol.co.uk/images/localpeople/ugc-images/275775/binaries/Danny%201.JPG"width="595">  <img src="http://www.thisisbristol.co.uk/images/localpeople/ugc-images/275775/binaries/Danny%202.JPG"width="595">  Read more about the move <a href="http://www.thisissouthwales.co.uk/Danny-Graham-seals-Swansea-City-Sunderland/story-18014424-detail/story.html" target=new rel=nofollow class=llblue>here</a>.  <b> UPDATE 2.10PM: Sunderland </B> have officially signed <b> Swansea's </b> Danny Graham, the club has confirmed. He joins on a three-and-a-half-year deal for &#65533;5 million.   <b> UPDATE 2.05PM: Plymouth Argyle </b> striker Rhys Griffiths has left the club by mutual consent, it has been confirmed.   The Welshman was told last week by Argyle manager John Sheridan he was surplus to requirements, and has been released from his contract in order to seek first-team football.   According to <a href="http://www.pafc.co.uk/news/article/hwyl-fawr-rhys-630302.aspx" target=new rel=nofollow class=llblue>the club's official website:</a> "Everybody at Home Park wishes Rhys all the best for the future".   <B> UPDATE 1.45PM: Stoke's </B> pursuit of <B> Birmingham </B> goalkeeper Jack Butland could involve a couple of players, Sky Sports understands.  It emerged earlier today a bid had been launched, and it is now thought discussions involve the possibility of a couple of Stoke players, including Ryan Shotton, heading in the opposite direction on loan.  <b> UPDATE 1.40PM: Peterborough </B> forward George Boyd is undergoing a medical at <B>Nottingham Forest</B>, while the Reds have bid for <B> Wolves </B> defender Stephen Ward.  <B> UPDATE 1.30PM: Norwich City </B> are edging closer to a deal for <b> Birmingham City's </b> Curtis Davies, it is understood.   <b> UPDATE 1.15PM: Fulham </B> have finalised the loan signing of Urby Emanuelson from <b>AC Milan</b>. He will remain at the club until the end of the season.  <b> UPDATE 1.10PM: Colchester's </b> Tom Eastman has signed a two-and-a-half-year contract extension.   The defender's deal was due to expire in the summer.   <b> UPDATE 1.05PM: </b> Striker Rhys Griffiths has left <b>Plymouth Argyle</b>, the club has tweeted:  <img src="http://www.thisisbristol.co.uk/images/localpeople/ugc-images/275775/binaries/Rhys%20tweet.JPG"width="595">   <B> QPR </B> have completed the signing of Christopher Samba for a club record fee: <img src="http://www.thisisbristol.co.uk/images/localpeople/ugc-images/275775/binaries/QPR%20tweet.JPG"width="595">  The 28-year-old Congolese defender has penned a four-and-a-half year deal in W12, after QPR agreed an undisclosed club record fee with <B> Anzhi Makhachkala </B> for the player's signature.  <b> Stoke </b> have tabled a &#65533;4million bid for <b> Birmingham City </b> goalkeeper keeper Jack Butland, Sky Sports reports. <b> Newport County </b> midfielder Lee Evans has signed with <b> Wolverhampton Wanderers </b> for an undisclosed fee. <b> Fleetwood Town </b> goalkeeper Chris Maxwell has returned to <b> Wrexham </b> on loan until the end of the season. <B> Burton Albion </B> have extended Adam Drury's loan spell from Premier League champions Manchester City. <p><img src="http://www.thisisbristol.co.uk/images/localpeople/ugc-images/275775/Article/images/18013387/4526511-thumb.jpg" alt="Transfer window deadline day: evening updates" title="Transfer window deadline day: evening updates"/></p><p><a href="http://www.acmewire.com/member/196983/">discoverer of the electron</a> </p>
