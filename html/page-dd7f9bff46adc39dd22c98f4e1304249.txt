						<h2>Unitrin Rescues Stranded Customers</h2>
<p>Unitrin Specialty now offers a roadside assistance program in most states. Don’t be stranded on the side of the road again. Ask for Roadside Assistance when you get your policy from Unitrin. The program is as follows:</p>
<p>One toll-free call gives customers nationwide access to these services:</p>
<ul>
<li>Emergency towing (15 miles)</li>
<li>Essential fluid and supplies</li>
<li>Lost key and lock-out service</li>
<li>Flat tire service</li>
<li>Dead battery service</li>
<li>Winching/Extrication</li>
</ul>
<p>Sign and drive – customers usually pay nothing when service is provided</p>
<p>No claim forms to fill out</p>
<p>Only $7 dollars per month covers ALL vehicles on the policy</p>
<p>Roadside Assistance offered through Unitrin Specialty provides customers the flexibility to pay their premiums monthly instead of up-front like other auto club programs. Plus, you won’t need to rewrite the Roadside Assistance application. It will be included automatically with your policy renewal.</p>
<p>&#8212;</p>
<p> The Unitrin family of insurance companies serves clients in markets across the United States.  More than 6 million policyholders have their basic insurance and financial needs met by a nationwide network of career agents and independent agents and brokers.  As one of America’s leading financial services providers, the Unitrin family specializes in property, casualty, life, health and accident insurance.</p>
