							<tr vAlign="top">
								<td><font face="Arial"><br>
								&nbsp;by: <b class="author">Jason Bibb</b> </font>
								<p><font face="Arial">If you want to turn your 
								regular car into a highway beast, all you need 
								are some good performance parts. You can boost 
								your horsepower, sprints and top speed by 
								installing the right parts. And the nice thing 
								about such upgrades is that you can get them for 
								virtually any car model or manufacturer. Let's 
								have a look at the most common performance part 
								upgrades: </font></p>
								<p><font face="Arial">Air filter intake </font>
								</p>
								<p><font face="Arial">Cold air intake filter 
								systems turn your average car into a real beast, 
								allowing the engine to develop more power in all 
								situations. Try to purchase street legal air 
								filter intakes and not get in trouble with the 
								law. Installing a performance cold air intake 
								gives our car up to 15 HP, a change that you 
								will definitely notice in city and highway 
								situations. </font></p>
								<p><font face="Arial">Performance Exhaust 
								Mufflers </font></p>
								<p><font face="Arial">Not only to they offer 
								better power but they also produce an amazing 
								sound, distinctive to select racing machines. 
								Exhaust mufflers and headers will transform your 
								car into a great looking customized driving 
								machine. But they are not all looks - they also 
								add up to 20 HP to your vehicle, regardless if 
								it's a sedan or SUV. </font></p>
								<p><font face="Arial">Springs, Shocks, 
								Suspension </font></p>
								<p><font face="Arial">Power is great but if you 
								do not have control over it the ride can turn 
								dangerous. There are several things you can do 
								to improve your car's handling and stability - 
								such as like lowering springs, adding shock 
								absorbers and installing a high performance 
								suspension system. When installing such 
								upgrades, your car clearance will be lower - 
								this has an excellent visual effect as well as 
								better airflow dynamics, which means excellent 
								handling and grip. </font></p>
