<h2>VIP Mercedes G Wagon</h2>
<p>The indomitable and powerful Mercedes G-Wagon was re-trimmed by us to create the ultimate macho interior – unlike any other G-Wagon interior on the road.</p>
<p>We completely re-modeled the interior – including specialist sports seats and the entire driver’s cabin. In our view the customer made an excellent choice of black/light grey leather seats and grey alcantara center strip – with contrasting white stitching to seat and back panels.</p>
<p>The design includes an uprated audio/multimedia system complete with touch screen controls and a small discreet refrigerator.</p>
