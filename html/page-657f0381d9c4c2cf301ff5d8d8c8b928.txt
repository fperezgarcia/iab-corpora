	<p align="justify">
    
	  <h1 align="justify">Creating Custom Convertible Tops</h1>
                        <p align="justify">By definition, a  convertible is nothing but an automobile which has a flexible roof. This  flexible &amp; attachable roof is named a convertible top. By retracting &amp;  folding the roof, this normal, enclosed car can be converted to an open air  vehicle. </p>
                        <p align="justify"><strong><u>Basic Mechanism of a Convertible Top: </u></strong></p>
                        <p align="justify">The roof is normally  fastened to the body of the vehicle &amp; it is non-detachable. The roof gets  folded away into a space behind the rear seats. The roof is operated manually  or automatically. <strong><a href="http://www.topsandseats.com/">Convertible tops</a></strong> are made of either by soft material like  vinyl or canvas or by some tough materials like steel, aluminum &amp; plastic.  Vehicle having <strong>soft top</strong> is called a roadster or cabrio. Tough material  automobiles are named coupe cabriolet. </p>
                        <div align="center"><img src="http://www.topsandseats.com/store/images/P/1949-Buick.jpg" width="276" height="222" /></div>
                        <p align="justify"><strong><u>To Maintain a Convertible Top: </u></strong></p>
                        <p align="justify"><strong>Convertible tops</strong> get  damaged due to sun&rsquo;s rays. Fading, staining, cracking &ndash; these are the negative  effects normally one faces. Some tops which are sensitive require long-lasting  coatings. For tops with <strong>plastic windows</strong>, cleaning at car wash or at home is  suggested. For home cleaning, soap &amp; water are to be used, to be followed  by, spraying &amp; cleaning with wax. Discoloration is a possibility due to wax  cleaning. So, it is better to be avoided. The convertible tops having glass  windows can be cleaned using normal glass cleaners. A razor blade can be used  for removing debris. Vacuum cleaners also serve the purpose. </p>
                        <p align="justify"><strong>Cars</strong> with convertible  tops were much in demand in 1980&rsquo;s. Men from all walks of life, wanted to own  these cars. Today, youngsters are more fascinated by these types of cars with  convertible tops. A convertible car, despite being a luxury car, is liked by  everyone. Sitting in the car, enjoying the feel of the sunshine, wafting  through the gust of wind, relishing the metallic band emerging from the stereo,  admiring the natural landscape on the highways, sniffing the salubrious breeze  away from the cities &ndash; a real pleasure to cherish.</p>
                        <p align="justify">Your desires &amp;  demands will decide the kin of vehicle you like. There are 3 kinds of tops - <strong>Vinyl  convertible top</strong>, <strong>canvas</strong> &amp; <strong>hard tops convertible</strong>. There are some  subcategories also. A car specialist will be of much use in your  decision-making process. You should remember the year &amp; the make of the  model. These minor details will be required to finalize about the suitable top  of your car. For instance, the cars of one era may require a particular service  which will be different from the service offered to the cars from another  period. So, it is imperative that you remember these basic details about the  year &amp; make of the model. </p>
                        <p align="justify">After installing the  top on your car, you have to think about maintaining it well. Your involvement  in the upkeep of the car will make the car serve you for a longer period.  <strong>Convertible tops</strong> made of vinyl or canvas will be susceptible to the impact of  elements of the road. Dirt patches, dust particles, bird droppings, climate  impact &ndash; the convertible top becomes a victim to all these factors. A <strong>car</strong>  detailer will help you in the maintenance of the car. You can also strive to put  forth same effort. To avoid scratches, you should not use brush. </p>
                        <p align="justify">Convertible top gives  an aristocratic look. In the absence of care &amp; maintenance, it won&rsquo;t last  long. Sunlight causes more damage to the top. Rear window suffers the damage  initially. Scratches &amp; discoloration damage the rear windows. The following  products &ndash; soft sponge or brush, vinyl window cleaner, convertible top  protectant breathable <strong>car cover</strong>, soft terry cloth towel &amp; clothing lint  roller. </p>
                        <p align="justify">The below-mentioned ways &amp; means will be useful tips for  maintaining your convertible tops. </p>
                        <div align="justify">
                          <ul type="disc">
                            <li>Speciality       cleaners have to be used on soft tops. </li>
      <li>Washing       the <strong>tops</strong> comes next.</li>
      <li>It       should not be done in sunlight. </li>
      <li>For       plastic windows, separate cleaners &amp; polishes have to be applied.</li>
      <li>After       washing it with plain water, allow it to dry. </li>
      <li>Don&rsquo;t       fold it. </li>
      <li>To       preserve color &amp; texture, use a top protectant after cleaning.</li>
      <li>Bird       droppings should be removed immediately as they are acidic. </li>
      <li>Snow       accumulating on the top is to be prevented. Too much snow will bend the       metal frame. </li>
      <li>Park       your vehicles in garages or car ports.</li>
      <li>Place       a soft towel or baby blanket over the plastic rear window. </li>
    </ul><br />
