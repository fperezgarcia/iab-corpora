<h1>Buying and Selling a Car in South Africa</h1>
<h2>Information on the paperwork and processes involved when buying a new car in South Africa;Â the documents required and where they must be sent...</h2>

<p>It is relatively straightforward to buy a car in South Africa, either as a resident or as a non-resident. Cars can be bought privately, from a dealership or an auction. VAT is charged on taxable goods supplied by a trader, such as a car dealer, but this tax is normally included in the sale price. All cars purchased in South Africa must be registered in South Africa.</p>

<h5>Buying a New Car</h5>

<p>New cars can be bought from dealers or online from websites such as <a class="external" target="_blank" href="/r.asp?http://www.autotrader.co.za/"><strong>Autotrader</strong></a>. VAT, currently at 14 percent, is included in the sale price of new cars bought from dealerships.</p>

<ul>
	<li>For the Autotrader guide to buying new cars:<a class="external" target="_blank" href="/r.asp?http://www.autotrader.co.za/includes/channels/cars/buy/new-cars.jsp"><strong> Click here</strong></a> </li>
</ul>
<h4>Registering a new vehicle</h4>

<p>To register a new vehicle it is necessary to go to the nearest motor vehicle registration authority with the following documents:</p>

<ul>
	<li>Proof of ID (such as a passport or identity card) </li>
	<li>The manufacturer's certificate with details about the vehicle </li>
	<li>A roadworthy certificate (if the vehicle is a heavy load) </li>
	<li>The completed application form for registering and licencing a motor vehicle (RLV). To download a copy of the RLV form: <a class="external" target="_blank" href="/r.asp?http://www.capegateway.gov.za/other/2006/3/rlv_app_registration_licence_motor_vehicle.pdf"><strong>Click here</strong></a> (PDF) </li>
	<li>Fee </li>
</ul>

<p>The fee for registering a vehicle depends on the province in which it is registered.</p>

<ul>
	<li>For more details about registering a vehicle and the fees involved: <a class="external" target="_blank" href="/r.asp?http://www.services.gov.za/services/content/Home/ServicesForPeople/Driving/Vehicleregistration/registeramotorvehicle/en_ZA"><strong>Click here</strong></a> </li>
