					<h1 class="pageTitle"><p>Get Emergency Roadside Assistance When
<br />
You Need It</p></h1>

				<article class="body-text   col-1">

          <p>It can be a major inconvenience when you really need to get somewhere and you’ve got a flat tire. Or you’re out of gas. Or your car just won’t start. Nationwide Roadside Assistance, available in select states, is like having your own personal pit crew 24/7. If you need help with a flat tire, a jump or a tow, our emergency Roadside Assistance program can help.</p>
						<h2>How Nationwide Roadside Assistance works</h2>
						<p>Once you sign up for Roadside Assistance, simply call <strong>1-800-421-3535</strong> when you need help.</p>
						<ul>
							<li>A service representative will confirm your location and send emergency, police or fire services if you’re in any danger.</li>
							<li>A Roadside Assistance field representative will be dispatched to your location to help.</li>
						</ul>
						<p>In many cases, you’ll just sign and drive – no payment is required.</p>
						<h2>Choose the Roadside Assistance program that fits your needs</h2>
						<p>Two levels of emergency Roadside Assistance coverage are available, so you can select the level of service that best meets your budget.</p>
						<h2>Nationwide Roadside Assistance Basic</h2>
						<p>This coverage applies to everyone on your policy, in any car you might occupy. </p>
						<ul>
							<li>We’ll provide a tow up to 15 miles.<sup>1</sup></li>
							<li>Our ditch extraction service pulls you out and gets you rolling again. (Some restrictions apply.)</li>
							<li>We’ll bring you emergency fuel if your tank is empty. (Some restrictions apply.)</li>
							<li>If your battery is dead, we’ll jump-start it back to life.</li>
							<li>We’ll cover lockout service up to $100.</li>
							<li>Flat tire? We’ll replace it with your spare to get you rolling again.</li>
						</ul>
						<h2>Nationwide Roadside Assistance Plus</h2>
						<p>This plan includes all the services above, plus:</p>
						<ul>
							<li>We’ll give you a tow, up to 100 miles.<sup>1</sup></li>
							<li>You’ll get trip interruption coverage that will reimburse your expenses up to $500<sup>2</sup> for alternate transportation, lodging and meals when you're stranded 100 or more miles from home.<sup>3</sup></li>
						</ul>    </article><article class="body-text  withDisclosure  col-1">
<aside class="disclosure">
          <p>1. Or $75 worth of coverage for Basic and $300 worth of coverage for Plus for Virginia. Or $100 worth of coverage for Basic for North Carolina.
<br />
2. $600 for Virginia policyholders.
<br />
3. Expenses covered up to 72 hours from time of disablement and shall not exceed $100/day for meals and lodging.</p>
						<p>24-hour Roadside Assistance services are provided by or through Agero.</p>
						<p>Insurance terms, definitions and explanations are intended for informational purposes only and do not in any way replace or modify the definitions and information contained in individual insurance contracts, policies or declaration pages, which control coverage determinations. Such terms may vary by state, and exclusions may apply.</p></aside>    </article>
