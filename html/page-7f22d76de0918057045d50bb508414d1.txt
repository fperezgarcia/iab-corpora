<p>Progressive's Emergency Roadside Assistance coverage helps give you peace of mind. If you would be interested in having this valuable coverage on your policy, talk to your Progressive agent. </p>

<h2>What's Included?</h2>
<p>You can purchase this coverage with your Progressive policy. Its available 24/7 &#8212; just call 1-888-776-2778 at any time &#8212; and a representative will dispatch a roadside service professional to help you with the following services:</p>
<ul class="productList" style="margin-left:30px;">
	<li><span style="font-weight:bold;">Towing &#8212;</span> Whenever your vehicle requires more than at-the-scene service, you can use your Roadside Assistance coverage to have it towed anywhere within a 15-mile radius, or to the nearest qualified repair facility if one doesn't exist within 15 miles.<br /><br /></li>
	<li><span style="font-weight:bold;">Flat Tire Change &#8212;</span> No need to panic if you have a flat. A roadside professional can put the spare tire on your vehicle at the scene.<br /><br /></li>
	<li><span style="font-weight:bold;">Battery Jump-Start &#8212;</span> Call us if your battery fails. If possible, it will be jump-started at the scene to help get you on your way.<br /><br /></li>
	<li><span style="font-weight:bold;">Emergency Fuel and Fluid Delivery &#8212;</span> If you run out of gas or your vehicle overheats, get the required fuel or fluids you need delivered directly to your vehicle. You only pay for the cost of the fuel or fluids. The delivery and service is free.<br /><br /></li>
	<li><span style="font-weight:bold;">Locksmith Service &#8212;</span> If your keys are lost, stolen or accidentally locked inside your vehicle, we will dispatch a qualified service provider. You only pay for the cost of the keys.<br /><br /></li>
</ul>
<br />
<h2>How Do You Use It?</h2>
<p>If you have Roadside Assistance coverage on your policy, the easiest way to use it is to call us at 1-800-776-2778. We'll send a service provider to you to get you up and running as soon as possible. Once the provider takes care of your car, you just sign the invoice and drive away. We pay the bill, and you pay nothing out of pocket (unless, of course, you request work that isn't related to the break down).</p>

<p><span style="font-style:italic;">Note</span>: In some states, such as North Carolina and Virginia, Roadside Assistance coverage is subject to limits stated on your policy. Roadside Assistance is not available on Progressive Commercial Auto policies. </p>
<p>If you contacted and paid for a Roadside Assistance service provider on your own, please send your receipt/invoice to Progressive Roadside Assistance, PO Box 9147, Medford, Mass., 02155. You'll need to include your name, policy number, the year/make/model of the vehicle that was serviced and a return address. Once we verify your coverage is in order, we'll reimburse you for your expenses, as long as they're within coverage limits. </p>

<h2>That Easy?</h2>
<p>Yep. That easy. Whether you're in your own driveway or on the road, a Roadside Assistance customer service representative is ready to help. We'll send you some company right away.</p>
