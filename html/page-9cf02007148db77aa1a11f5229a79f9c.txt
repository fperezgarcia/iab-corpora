Our appraisals&nbsp; are
recognized and accepted by the courts, legal community, insurance
industry,
major lending institutions and the general public since its inception.
The
values expressed by this company are independent in that we are not
under
contract to any law firm, financial institution, insurance company or
any other
public or private agency. The value derived for any specific vehicle is
based
on years of experience with comparable market surveys, dealer surveys

inquiries, price guide interpretations, added market value adjustments
for
custom or modified vehicles and my own personal sales and marketing
experience.

an
estimated actual
cash value given the year, make, model, condition, options, history and
desirability of the subject vehicle in relation to its standing among
other
similar vehicles within the marketplace. As is the current standard
values are
based on market samplings with adjustments for mileage, condition and
options
to better reflect the specific value of the subject vehicle. Other
methods may be
employed including&nbsp;added-value computations for custom or
upgraded vehicles, dealer quotes and manufacturers inquiries if deemed
appropriate for any given vehicle or situation<br>
