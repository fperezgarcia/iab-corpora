	    <h1>Roadside Assistance and Towing Coverage</h1>
	    <p class="posted-in">Posted in  
	        	        	            	            	            <a rel="category tag" title="View all posts in Auto Insurance" href="http://www.goinsurancerates.com/auto-insurance/">Auto Insurance</a>
	            	        	            	            , 	            <a rel="category tag" title="View all posts in Auto Insurance Riders" href="http://www.goinsurancerates.com/auto-insurance-riders/">Auto Insurance Riders</a>
	            	        	        	    </p>
	    <p class="the-time">
	        January 14th, 2009	    </p>
	    <p class="the-content"><p>If you own a car, you should really invest in <strong>roadside assistance and towing coverage</strong>. This will allow you to call your insurance company if your car breaks down, and youre unable to drive it. A tow truck will come to get you and the car, towing it to an auto mechanics garage where it can be assessed. This coverage could really come in handy if you damage your vehicle and need assistance. <span id="more-149559"></span></p>
<p>For example, say youre driving across country from Boston to Los Angeles in your minivan. Everything is going smoothly until you get to a small town at the far end of Kansas.. As you enter the city limits you smell something strange  and then you immediately notice that theres smoke coming from your engine. The engine light comes on and your car dies. Now youre out in the middle of nowhere, and its a five-mile walk into town in the pouring rain. What do you do? <strong>With <a href="http://www.goinsurancerates.com/auto-insurance/towing-and-labor-coverage/">roadside assistance and towing coverage</a>, you can make a simple call and wait for help to arrive</strong>. Soon, someone will be on their way to get you and your car and bring you to a mechanics garage, where the problem with your car can be assessed. If you didnt have roadside assistance and towing coverage, youd have to walk the five miles into Wakeenie in the pouring rain, find a repair station, and then have them bring you back to your car. What if you were traveling with a pet? With roadside assistance and towing coverage, youd be rescued without ever having to leave your car or your loved ones. Whats more, its all already paid for, meaning you wont have to spend a dime. </p>
<p>As with everything, policies can vary from company to company, so when it comes to roadside assistance and towing coverage, be sure to explore all your options with an auto insurance specialist before you make any move. </p>
<p>Besides roadside assistance and towing coverage, you may want to explore other additions to your auto policy:</p>
