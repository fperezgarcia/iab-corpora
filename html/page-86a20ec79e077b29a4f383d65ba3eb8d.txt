    <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="entry-body">
            <p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-family: verdana,geneva; font-size: 10pt;"><strong>Welcome to the free Chicago Art Fair Calendar, offering info on the top juried Art Festivals and Craft Shows in Chicago and Chicagoland for all seasons in&nbsp;</strong><strong>Naperville, Oak Park, Wilmette, Evanston, Downers Grove, Skokie, Highland Park, Oak Brook, Batavia, Geneva, Northbrook, LaGrange, and more!</strong></span></p>
