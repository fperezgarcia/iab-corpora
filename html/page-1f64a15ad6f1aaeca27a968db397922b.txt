<p>Infiniti announced that it will use the 2013 Detroit<a target="_blank" title=" auto show" href=" http://www.motortrend.com/auto_shows/"> auto show</a> to reveal an all-new sports sedan &#8212; but the company won&#8217;t say what the car will be. The debut will most likely be the next Infiniti G sedan, but there&#8217;s also a chance it could be the company&#8217;s front-wheel-drive sedan that uses a Mercedes-Benz platform.</p>
<p><a target="_blank" href="http://wot.motortrend.com/infiniti-launching-new-luxury-sports-sedan-at-2013detroit-auto-show-296303.html/2014-infiniti-g-right-front-spy-shots-2-2" rel="attachment wp-att-296333"><img class="alignright size-medium wp-image-296333" title="2014 Infiniti G right front spy shots 2 300x187 photo" src="http://stwot.motortrend.com/files/2012/11/2014-Infiniti-G-right-front-spy-shots-2-300x187.jpg" alt="2014 Infiniti G right front spy shots 2 300x187 image" width="300" height="187" /></a>We know that Infiniti is hard at work revamping its G sedan, and we&#8217;ve even <a href="http://wot.motortrend.com/spied-next-gen-infiniti-g-sedan-testing-near-the-nurburgring-272761.html">spied a camouflaged test car</a> running in Germany. The current car (pictured) hasn&#8217;t had a major revamp since its introduction in 2007, and it desperately needs upgrades to stay competitive with European rivals like the BMW <a title="3-Series" href=" BMW 3-Series http://www.motortrend.com/new_cars/04/bmw/3_series/">3-Series</a>. Rumors suggest the<a target="_blank" title=" new car" href=" http://www.motortrend.com/"> new car</a> will use an updated version of its current 3.7-liter V-6 engine, with the potential for a Mercedes-sourced engine as an option. The top-spec G37 IPL could become a much more aggressive performance machine, possibly housing a <a target="_blank" href="http://www.motortrend.com/future/future_vehicles/1204_infiniti_super_g_with_530_hp_twin_v6_in_pipeline/">twin-turbo V-6 engine with as much as 530 hp</a>. After the sedan debuts, Infiniti will also launch the familiar G coupe and G convertible.</p>
<p>Yet most reports suggest the Infiniti G will make its public debut at the Geneva Motor Show in the spring, not at Detroit.That could mean that the January debut will instead be Infiniti&#8217;s front-drive luxury car.</p>
<p>Infiniti previously confirmed that it will build a compact car, loosely based on the Etherea concept (pictured below), <a href="http://wot.motortrend.com/confirmed-new-infiniti-compact-will-use-mercedes-benz-platform-engines-117541.html">on a Mercedes platform</a>. That will likely be the MFA front-wheel-drive architecture used for cars like the new A- and B-Class. Designed to rival the BMW 1-Series and Audi A3, the compact Infiniti is hoped to be a big hit in Europe &#8212; Infiniti reportedly hopes the car would account for 40 percent of its annual sales there. A selection of gasoline and diesel four-cylinder Mercedes engines will power the car.</p>
<p>If Infiniti follows the trends of most other automakers, we can expect to see teaser photos and/or videos emerge over the next few weeks before the Detroit auto show. That should give us a better idea of exactly which sports sedan the luxury brand will introduce in January.</p>
