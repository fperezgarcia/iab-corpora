      <p>The Department of Fine Arts and Art History seeks to interweave creative expression, art theory, and history. The strong partnership between the <a href="programs/FA_programs/FA_program.php">Fine Arts</a> and <a href="programs/AH_programs/AH_program.php">Art History</a> promotes our critical approach, which is grounded in both contemporary and historical concerns. We challenge our students to think beyond the classroom, to interpret the arts within a broader visual culture, treating the classroom as a testing ground for their ideas. </p>
      <p>The Department offers Bachelor of Arts degrees in Fine Arts, Art History, or a combination of the two as well as a Master of Fine Arts and a Master of Arts in Art History. Undergraduate and graduate students have a range of options for studying the visual and creative arts and for developing visual literacy as well as critical, analytic, and research skills. </p>
