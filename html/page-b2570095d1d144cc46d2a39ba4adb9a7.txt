                  <h1>Hybrid Vehicle Training</h1>
                  
                  
    

                   



                   

  

                  <div id="node-1543" class="node clear-block">

  <div class="meta">
  
    </div>


  <div class="content clear-block"><div class="content-inner">
    <h3>Learn the latest hybrid technology! </h3><p><strong>Learn to service sophisticated hybrid vehicles and earn a Green Technician Certificate upon successful completion.</strong></p><p>You will get:</p><ul><li>A state-of-the-art education on the safe-service and repair of eight hybrid vehicles</li><li>The skill to service and convert regular vehicles into plug-in electric hybrid vehicles</li><li>Experience with diagnostics and troubleshooting of advanced technology vehicles</li><li>Expert training from ASE master-certified technicians</li></ul><h4>Schedule</h4><p><strong>Three two-day modules are offered:</strong><br><br><strong>Module 1</strong> – <a title="Module 1 – Hybrid Safety and Theory" href="/departments-and-programs/a-z-programs/automotive-technology/hybrid-vehicle-training/hybrid-vehicle">Hybrid Safety and Theory<br></a><strong>Module 2</strong> – <a title="Module 2 – Plug-in Hybrid Conversion" href="/departments-and-programs/a-z-programs/automotive-technology/hybrid-vehicle-training/hybrid-vehicle">Plug-in Hybrid Conversion<br></a><strong>Module 3</strong> – <a title="Module 3 – Diagnostics and Troubleshooting of Advanced" href="/departments-and-programs/a-z-programs/automotive-technology/hybrid-vehicle-training/hybrid-vehicle">Diagnostics and Troubleshooting of Advanced Technology Vehicles</a></p><p><strong>Check back in later for the spring and summer Hybrid Vehicle Training schedule.</strong></p>

<p><br><strong>FEE: $350 per Module:</strong> Includes instruction, lab fees, two lunches, breaks and a $50 manual. If two or more from the same organization attend any one program, all get a <strong>10% discount</strong>. <strong>ASA members</strong> will also get a $30-discount for each registration.</p><p><strong>LOCATION:</strong> Arapahoe Community College 5900 S. Santa Fe Drive, Littleton</p><p><strong><a href="https://ssl.boulder.net/arapahoe/cld-registration-form.html">REGISTER ONLINE TODAY</a></strong> or Call 303.734.3701 to earn your <strong>Green Technician Certificate</strong> in Hybrid Vehicle Technology.</p>  </div></div> <!-- /.content-inner, /.content -->
