          <div class="article">
   	            <p>A rough idle or excessive vibration from the engine compartment may be experienced with some 2012 Chrysler Caravan/Town and Country vehicles. Some vehicles may experience a low pressure fuel line vibration and a possible rough idle.</p>
<p>Fuel pressure pulsation in the low pressure fuel line system may cause a vibration noise or rough idle.</p>
<p>When removing the existing fuel rail return line, place some absorbent material under each fitting before removing, to catch the diesel fuel that will drain out.</p>
<ol start="1">
<li>Remove the engine cover by pulling straight up.</li>
<li>Remove the two plastic clips that attach the injector fuel return line to the rail fuel return line and discard the original clips.</li>
<li>Remove the fuel rail return line by disengaging the quick-connect fittings at both ends of the line and discard the original line.</li>
<li>Install the new rail return line by pushing the quick-connect fittings squarely on until they click, and pull back to ensure that the fitting is latched properly.</li>
<li>Install the two new fuel return line clips to attach the injector fuel return line to the rail fuel return line in the original locations.</li>
<li>Start the engine and allow to idle for 60 seconds while checking the connections for leaks.</li>
<li>Reinstall the engine cover.</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>PARTS REQUIRED</strong></p>
