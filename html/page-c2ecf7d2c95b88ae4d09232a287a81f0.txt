					<h2>Overview of the AL650 Auto Hybrid Platform Power Wheelchair</h2>
					<p>With a weight capacity of 350 lbs, and a platform that extends up to 50&rdquo; in length&ndash; the AL650 can accommodate virtually any powerchair. This hybrid lift combines the ease of an outdoor platform with the security of an inside lift. One great feature of this lift is that it requires no drilling or modification to your vehicle. The lift installs in your vehicle using the existing 3rd row seating. <br />
<br />
Once the lift is properly installed, you simply drive your powerchair onto the platform, and it automatically locks into the platform&rsquo;s docking station. Some modification is required to make your chair compatible with the docking station, but it eliminates the need for straps - so transportation is quicker and easier than ever. The AL650 is also fully automatic; it lifts and retracts into the vehicle in seconds using the standard remote hand control. <br />
<br />
The AL650 will work with many popular powerchairs, and can be installed in the cargo area of many SUVs, mini-vans, and full-size vans. This is one of the most convenient, secure, and dependable lifts on the market today.<br />
