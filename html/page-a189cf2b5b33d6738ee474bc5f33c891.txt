           <h2 class="summary title_gray" style="margin-bottom:5px;">ONLY Convertible Car Seat Under $100 that gets Excellent Crash Rating from Consumer Reports</h2>
           
           <span class="iReviewStarsMedYellow medStars50 rating"><span class="value-title" title="5"></span></span>
           
           <br>
           <span class='dtreviewed rgr'>Jul 25, 2004<span class="value-title" title="2004-07-25"></span></span> 
           <div style="margin-top:5px; color:#666;"><a href="/member/show_~news/sub_~ratingsguidelines">Rated</a> a <span style="color:#000;">Very Helpful Review</span> by the Epinions community</div>

            <div class="review_details">
               <ul class="user_review_chart">
          <li><h3 style="display:inline-block; float:left;"><span class="rgr" style="display:inline-block; font-weight:bold; margin:0 5px 5px 0;">User Rating: </span><span class="rgr">Excellent</span></h3></li>

           <div class="user_review_summary">
          
             
             <h3 style="display:inline-block; margin-bottom:3px;"><b style="display:inline-block; margin-right:5px;">Pros:</b>Outstanding value, impressive padding and great safety ratings<br /></h3>
             <h3 style="display:inline-block; margin-bottom:3px;"><b style="display:inline-block; margin-right:5px;">Cons:</b>May be too small for certain children, rear-adjusting straps, no recline<br /></h3>
             

             
             <h3 style="display:inline-block; margin-bottom:3px;"><b style="display:inline-block; margin-right:5px;">The Bottom Line: </b>This is an outstanding convertible with uncompromised safety features at an unbelievable price point.</h3><br /><br />
             
          

          
                </div>
            </div>
        <div class="user_review_full">
           <p class="description plot_details">
          
          Let me just start off by getting right to the point: the <a href="/Century_1500_STE_Car_Seat_Car_Seats">Century 1500 STE Convertible Car Seat</a> really <i>does</i> make the perfect second car seat, particularly if you are on a budget.  It's affordable, it's comfortable and it's safe.  It&#146s <i>so safe that it is the ONLY convertible under $100 that receives an excellent for a crash rating.</i> On the other hand, it&#146s got some design peculiarities that may not make it the perfect seat for all children.  At the same time, <i>don't throw the baby out with the bath water!</i>  <br>
<br>
I would <i>highly</i> recommend this seat if you are looking for an inexpensive but safe second car seat to accommodate a baby or smaller toddler.  If you are planning to have another child, this may be a seat that be passed down once your growing toddler is ready for a booster seat.  Because of the outstanding price point and great padding on this seat, I would definitely recommend taking a look!  <br>
<br>
<br>
<b>PRODUCT DESCRIPTION</b><br>
The <a href="/Century_1500_STE_Car_Seat_Car_Seats">Century 1500 STE Convertible Car Seat</a> is a convertible car seat produced by <b>Graco</b>.  For those who may be unfamiliar with car seat terminology, a "convertible" car seat is positionable in either the forward-facing or rear-facing modes to accommodate the safety needs of infants and toddlers.  <br>
<br>
*Harness: It&#146s got a 5-point harness with straps that can be positioned at three shoulder levels.  The metal clips are simple (no IQ tests here) and easy to insert into the clip base.  A red button can be pressed with one-hand to release the clips.  <br>
<br>
*Straps: They are a little wider than some of the other straps I&#146ve seen on the market.  This width may prevent them from getting tangled.  I&#146ve been pleasantly surprised with the fact that really don&#146t get tangled very easily at all.  This chest clasp is good.  I think this particular <b>Graco</b> clasp releases more easily than the <b>Evenflo</b> clasp.<br>
<br>
*Seat frame: The seat frame is all plastic and positioned at a comfortable angle to allow a child remain either alert or asleep.  Unfortunately, this seat does not recline which may make it suboptimal for long car rides or long commutes but for ordinary use it is just perfect.<br>
<br>
*Padding: The padding on this seat is just OUTSTANDING for the value.  It&#146s full and cushy to really hug a small child nicely.  The fabric is soft, attractive and machine washable.  Also, a plush little pillow has been provided for added comfort in the head and neck.  I couldn&#146t be happier with the quality of the fabric and padding for the price point.<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>WHAT ARE THE WEIGHT OR SIZE LIMITS FOR THIS CAR SEAT? </b><br>
The manufacturer recommends using the <a href="/Century_1500_STE_Car_Seat_Car_Seats">Century 1500 STE Convertible Car Seat</a>  rear-facing only for infants up to 22lbs and forward-facing for children 20 to 40lbs and less than 40 inches tall.  <br>
<br>
<br>
<b>HOW DO INDEPENDENT ORGANIZATIONS RATE THIS SEAT?</b><br>
* National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) Ratings:<br>
Assembly - A <br>
Evaluation of Labels Rating - B <br>
Evaluation of Instructions Rating - B <br>
Installing Features Rating - A <br>
Securing the Child Rating - B<br>
Overall Ease of Use Rating - B <br>
<br>
* Consumer Reports Ratings:<br>
Crash Protection &#150 Excellent to Very Good <br>
Ease of Use &#150 Fair to Very Good <br>
Fit to Vehicle &#150 Good to Excellent <br>
Notes about this particular rating from Consumer Reports &#150 This is the ONLY convertible under $100 that receives an excellent for a crash rating.  For more information on this seat and for great safety tips related to the installation of all car seats please go to: http://www.consumerreports.org/<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>HOW EASY IS IT TO INSTALL? </b><br>
The installation of this seat is a snap.  Because it&#146s not as bulky as some of the other seats out there, it is very easy to thread a seat belt through the space in the back.  You&#146ll also find that <b>Graco&#146s</b> convenient leveling device (a ball that falls within a green or red region depending on how the seat is positioned) will instantly let you know if the seat is angled appropriately within your vehicle.  It is also LATCH compatible which makes installation very straight-forward.<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>HOW EASY IS IT TO ADJUST?</b><br>
Because of the great price point on this seat, you&#146re going to have to adjust the straps from behind.  This means that you&#146ll need to remove the seat from the car to re-thread the straps at the various levels or to tighten at the same level.  It is definitely an inconvenience but I suppose something&#146s got to give for such an affordable seat.<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>OVERALL ASSESSMENT</b><br>
I now personally own five car seats and this is the newest seat added to our fleet.  I am extraordinarily pleased with the comfort and safety it provides for my child at a most reasonable $40-50 price.  Go <b>GRACO</b>!<br>
<br>
If you have any questions about this product, please feel free to contact me at any time.<br>
