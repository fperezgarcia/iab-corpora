					<h3>High Performance Powertrains</h3>
<p>The objective of our High Performance Powertrains concentration is to provide the student with up-to-date specialty training in many high-performance applications of diesel and automotive upgrades. Student will receive training theory, hands-on repair and diagnosis of modern hi-tech diesel vehicles and gasoline powered vehicles with an emphasis on performance.</p>
<h3>Course Objectives</h3>
<p>Students can apply themselves to learn:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Engine block design</li>
  <li>Rotating and reciprocating assemblies</li>
  <li>Performance camshaft and valvetrain variations</li>
  <li>Fuel injection management systems</li>
  <li>Ignition control systems</li>
  <li>Systems upgrades</li>
  <li>Electronic engine management system</li>
  <li>Computer reprogramming and calibration for outcome parameters</li>
  <li>Modifications and power train tuning for improved performance</li>
  <li>Chassis dynamometer verification of performance outcomes</li>
</ul>
<h3>Career Opportunities</h3>
<p>Career opportunities for students who specialize in high performance powertrains may include:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Engine Builder</li>
  <li>Street Rod Builder</li>
  <li>Race Team Technician</li>
  <li>Performance Engine Tuning</li>
  <li>Aftermarket Parts Research and Development</li>
</ul>		<br />
