Through the first 11 months of this year, Mercedes-Benz leads with 245,910 vehicles sold. (The total subtracts Mercedes' utility van business.) BMW is second at 244,061 vehicles. "The only major roadblock ahead for the U.S. market is the fiscal cliff.
