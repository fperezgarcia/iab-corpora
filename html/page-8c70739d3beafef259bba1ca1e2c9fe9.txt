		<h1 class="entry-header">Subaru to Debut First Hybrid at 2013 New York Auto Show</h1>
	
	<div class="entry-content">
		<div class="entry-body">
			<p>
<img alt="Xvhybrid" border="0" class="asset  asset-image at-xid-6a00d83451b3c669e2017ee9928c74970d" src="http://blogs.cars.com/.a/6a00d83451b3c669e2017ee9928c74970d-800wi" title="Xvhybrid" /></p>
Hybrid cars and SUVs have become so commonplace that it can be a surprise when an automaker doesn&#39;t have one in its lineup.
<p>Today, Subaru announced it will introduce its first hybrid at the 2013 New York International Auto Show next week. The Subaru XV Crosstrek Hybrid gets the honor of adding a more fuel-efficient option to the surprisingly popular XV Crosstrek, which is based on the Impreza hatchback.</p>
<p>
More information will be revealed next week about the hybrid&#39;s powertrain. Subaru also says it will debut a new concept performance car in New York. 
</p>
